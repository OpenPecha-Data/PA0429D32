dvitīya adhyāyaḥpūjāphalavarṇano nāmaatha dhīmān mahāsattvo maitreyaḥ sa jinātmajaḥ|bhagavantaṃ
punarnatvā sāñjalirevamabravīt

bhagavacchrotumicchāmi svayambhūtpatti satkathāṃ|tadbhavānsamupākhyātuṃ lokānāṃ saṃpravodhane

iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena maitreyeṇa sudhīmatā|bhagavānstān sabhālokān sampaśyannevamādiśat

sādhu maitreya sarve'pi sabhālokāḥ samādarāt|śṛṇudhvaṃ saṃpravakṣāmi svayambhūtpattisatkathāṃ

tadyathā nirvṛtiṃ yāte vipaśvini munīśvare|cirakālāntareṇātra jagacchāstābhavat punaḥ

śikhi nāma munīndro'rhan dharmarājastathāgataḥ|sarvajñaḥ sugataḥ sarvavidyādhipo vināyakaḥ

tadā saptativarṣāṇāṃ sahasrāryunṛṇāmabhūt|ahaṃ kṣemaṃkaro nāma bodhisattvo bhavaṃ kila

yadā sa bhagavāñchāstā śikhidharmādhipo jinaḥ|aruṇākhyapuropānte vyaharat saugatāśrame

tadā sarve mahāsattvā bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ|śikhinastasya śāstuḥ saṃprābhajan samupasthitāḥ|ahamapi
tathā tasya śikhinastrijagadguroḥ

śaraṇe samupasthāya prābhajaṃ sarvadā mudā|tatraika samaye so'rhan bhagavān sarvavicchikhī

saddharma samupādeṣṭuṃ sabhāsane samāśrayet|tadvīkṣya bhikṣavaḥ sarve śrāvakā brahmacāriṇaḥ

bhikṣuṇyo vratinaḥ sarve upāsakā upāsikāḥ|tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pātuṃ samupāgatya taṃ muniṃ

natvā sāñjalayastatra parivṛtya samāśrayet|tadā brahmāmarendrādi tridaśāḥ sarve āgatāḥ

sarve lokādhipāścāpi dharma śrotuṃ samāgatāḥ|siddhā vidyādharā sādhyā yakṣagandharvakinnarāḥ

garuḍā rākṣasendrāśca daityā nāgādhipā api|ṛṣayo brāhmaṇāścāpi tīrthikāścāpi tapasvinaḥ

yatayo yoginaścāpi nirgranthāśca digambarāḥ|rājāno kṣatriyā vaiśyā amātyā mantriṇo janāḥ

śilpino vaṇijaḥ sārthavāhādayo mahājanāḥ|paurā jānapadā grāmyāstathānyadeśavāsinaḥ

tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pātuṃ saṃharṣitāḥ samāgatāḥ|tatra te samupāgatya samīkṣya taṃ munīśvaraṃ

yathākramaṃ samabhyarcya kṛtvā cāpi pradakṣiṇāṃ|kṛtvāñjali puṭo natvā parivṛtya samantataḥ

puraskṛtya samudvīkṣya samādarādupāśrayan|tān sarvān samupāsīnāṃ dṛṣṭvā sa bhagavāñchikhī

ādimadhyāntakalyāṇaṃ saddharma samupādiśat|tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pītvā sarve lokāḥ pravodhitāḥ

saddharmasādhanodyuktā vabhūvu bodhibhāginaḥ|tasminneva samaye tatra puṇyajalāśraye hrade

maṇinālaṃ mahaddīptihīrakeśaramuttama|pañcaratnamayaṃ divyasarojarājakarṇṇikaṃ

prādurbhūtaṃ mahāpadmaṃ sahasradalakāśitaṃ|tasya ratnasarojasya karṇṇikāmadhyamaṇḍale

svayambhūtsamutpanno dharmmadhāturjinālayaḥ|ekahastapramāṇāṃśuḥ śubhraratnamayojjvalaḥ

sambodhiśrīguṇādhāraḥ sarvalakṣaṇamaṇḍitaḥ|jyotirūpo jagajjeṣṭha pañcatathāgatāśrayaḥ

jagadīśo jagat vandyo jagatpūjyo jagatprabhuḥ|anādinidhano'jīrṇṇo mānyaḥ sarvaśubhārthabhṛt

samantabhadrarūpo'graḥ śreṣṭhaḥ saddharmaratnabhṛt|trailokyasadguṇādhīśaścaturvarggaphalapradaḥ

tasmiṃścaitye samutpanne sāvdhinagā rasācalat

divyasugandhipuṣpāṇi saṃnipetuḥ surālayāt|suradundubhayo neduḥ diśaḥ sarvāḥ prasedire

vahnayo dakṣiṇāvarttā bhadrābhāḥ saṃprajahvalaḥ|suśītalāḥ sugandhyādyā dhīraṃ vavuḥ samīraṇāḥ

vavarṣuḥ surasāmbūni meghā gambhīranisvanāḥ|graha tārenducandrābhāḥ sabhāsitā virejire

stutimaṅgalasaṃgītiśabdā vyomni pracerire|sarvatrāpi sumāṅgalyamahotsāhaṃ nirantaraṃ

subhikṣaṃ śrīguṇorbhāvaṃ saddharma bhadrasādhanaṃ|nirutpātaṃ śubhācāraṃ prāvarttata samantataḥ

tamevaṃ svayambhūtpannaṃ dharmmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|samīkṣyeśādayaḥ sarve ārupya bhuvanāśritāḥ

yogadhyāna mahānanda saukhye'pi viratotsavāḥ|svayambhuvaṃ tamīśānaṃ vandituṃ samupācaran

tathā brahmādayaḥ sarve munayo brahmacāriṇaḥ|evaṃ svayambhuvaṃ draṣṭuṃ muditā samupācaran

evaṃ śakrādayaḥ sarve tridaśāḥ sāpsaro gaṇāḥ|pūjāṅgāni samādāya draṣṭumenaṃ mudā yayuḥ

tathāgnidharmarājo'pi naiṛto varuṇo marut|śrīdo bhūtādhipaścaivaṃ sarve lokādhipā api

svasvaparijanaiḥ sārddha mahotsāhaiḥ pramoditāḥ|evaṃ svayambhuvaṃ caityaṃ saṃdraṣṭuṃ samupāyayuḥ

dhṛtarāṣṭro mahārājo gandharvaiḥ saha moditaḥ|saṃgītivādanotsāhaiḥ sahainaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

viruḍhako mahārājaḥ kumbhāṇḍasahitā mudā|svayambhuvaṃ tamālokya mudā vanditumāyayuḥ

virupākṣo'pi nāgendraiḥ sarvai nāgādhipaiḥ saha|ratnapūjopahārāṇi dhṛtvainaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

kuvero yakṣarājo'pi yakṣaṇībhiḥ samanvitaḥ|nānādravyopahārāṇi dhṛtvainaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

vajrapāṇiśca guhyendrasarvaguhyakasaṃyutaḥ|divyabhogyāpahārāṇi dhṛtvainaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

drumaḥ kinnararājo'pi sarvaiḥ saha hyānanaiḥ|tūryasaṃghoṣaṇotsāhaiḥ sahinaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

tathā sarvārthasiddhākhyaḥ sarvavidyādharādhipaḥ|divyapūjopahārāṇi dhṛtvainaṃ draṣṭumāyayuḥ

garuḍaḥ pakṣirājo'pi sarvaiḥ pakṣigaṇaiḥ saha|svasvarddhiśrīmahotsāhairenaṃ saṃdraṣṭumāyayuḥ

evaṃ siddhāśca sādhyāśca vasavaśca grahā api|sarvāstārāganāścāpi sarvāścāpyapsarogaṇāḥ

evaṃ daityādhipāḥ sarve svasvaparijanaiḥ saha|mahāsamṛddhiprātsāhaiḥ sahasā samupācaran

evaṃ lokādhipāḥ sarve daśadikṣu vyavasthitāḥ|dṛṣṭvā taṃ svayambhūtpannaṃ vandituṃ sahasācaran

sarve'pi te samāgatya dṛṣṭvā taṃ jagadīśvaraṃ|saṃharṣitāśayā dūrāt praṇatvā samupācaran

tataste jagannātha sarve'bhyarcya yathākramaṃ|aṣṭāṃgaiḥ praṇatiṃ kṛtvā prābhajanta samādarāt

keciddavyasugandhaistaṃ prābhilipyābhajan mudā|kecinnānāvidhaiḥ puṣpaiḥ keciddhūpairmanoharaiḥ

kecicca puṣpamālābhiḥ kecicca divyacīvaraiḥ|kecicca dīpamālābhiḥ kecidāratidīpanaiḥ

keciddivyāmṛtaiḥ bhogyaiḥ keciddivyauṣadhairapi|kecinnānāvidhairdivyaratnālaṃkārābhūṣaṇaiḥ

kecicchatradhvajaivalivyajanaiśca vitānakaiḥ|kecit saṃgītisaṃvādyairmṛdaṅgamurajādibhiḥ

kecit tauryatrikai vaṃśaiḥ śaṃkhaiḥ śṛgaiśca kecana|kāhāraiśca tathā kecit vīṇādītatavādanaiḥ

tālādighanavādyaiśca bheryānakamardalaiḥ|tathā nānāvidhairmaḍḍurḍiṇḍimajharjharādibhiḥ

kecitnṛtyaiśca gītaiśca hāhākāraiśca kecana|evaṃ nānāvidhotsāhaiḥ prābhajanstaṃ jinālayaṃ

kecit pradakṣiṇānyeva kṛtvā bhajan sahasraśaḥ|kecicca dhāraṇīvidyājapastotrādibhirmudā

evaṃ nānāprakāraiste sarve lokā dvijādayaḥ|śraddhayā samupāśritya prābhajanstaṃ svayambhuvaṃ

etaddivyamahotsāhaiḥ saṃpravṛttiṃ prasāritāṃ|śrutvā sarve sabhālokā vismayaṃ samupāyayuḥ

tat samīkṣya mahāsattvā ratnapāṇiḥ purogataḥ|śikhinaṃ taṃ muniṃ natvā sāñjalirevamabravīt

bhagavan yatmahī sarvā kampitā divyamutsavaṃ|prasāritaṃ ca kasyedaṃ hetu tat samupādiśa

iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena sudhiyā ratnapāṇinā|sa śikhi bhagavān paśyan ratnapāṇiṃ tamabravīt

sādhu śṛṇu mahāsattva yat sarvā calitā mahī|divyotsāhaiḥ pravṛttaṃ ca taddhetuṃ saṃnigadyate

tadyathātrāsti bhūloka uttarasyāṃ himālaye|aṣṭāṅgaguṇasampannapuṇyajalāśrayo hradaḥ

tatra ratnamaye padme sarojarājakarṇṇike|svayameva samutpanno dharmadhātujinālayaḥ

tadutpanne mahī sarvā racitā saṃpramoditāḥ|sarvatrāpi śubhotsāhaṃ prāvrarttate bhavālaya

taṃ samīkṣya maheśānabrahmāśakrādayo'marāḥ|grahāstārāśca siddhāśca sādhyā vidyādharā api

maharṣaśca sarve'pi savismayapramoditāḥ|sarve lokādhipāścāpi daityā nāgāḥ khageśvarāḥ

yakṣakinnaragandharvvaguhyakarākṣasā apiḥ|etatprabhṛtayaḥ sarve samīkṣya taṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ

saṃharṣitāḥ samāgatya prabhajante mahotsavaiḥ|etatpūjāmahotsāhasaṃpravṛttiḥ prasārita

etasyedaṃ mahadbhanimittaṃ saṃprajāyate|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa śikhinā saṃnisaṃmya saḥ

ratnapāṇimahāsattvāḥ savismayapramoditaḥ|suprabuddhamukhāmbhojā mahāsaṃharṣitāśayaḥ

bhūyastaṃ śikhinaṃ natvā sāñjalirevabravīt|bhagavan taddhijānīyādyanmano me samīcchate

tadanujñāṃ pradatvātra saṃprasādayatumarhati||ahamapi jagacchāstaḥ svayambhuvaṃ tamīśvaraṃ

śraddhayā samupāśritya prabhajāmi mahotsavaiḥ||iti saṃprārthite tena śrutvā sa bhagavāñchikhi

ratnapāṇiṃ mahāsattvaṃ saṃpaśyannevamādiśat||kulaputra prayāhi tvaṃ yadyāśu bodhimicchasi

śraddhayā samupāśritya prabhaja taṃ jinālayaṃ|ye tatra samupāśritya bhajeyuḥ śraddhayā mudā

teāśu prāpya saṃbodhiṃ sambuddhapadamāpnuyuḥ|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa ratnapāṇi niśamya saḥ

sāñjalistaṃ muniṃ natvā muditaḥ prācarat tataḥ|tatastena sahāneke bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ

śrāvakā bhikṣavaścāpi bhikṣuṇyaścāpyupāsikāḥ|upāsakā bhaktimanto vratinaḥ puṇyalālasāḥ

ṛṣayo brāhmaṇāścāpi yatayo yogino'pi ca|tīrthikāstāpasāścāpi nirgranthā brahmacāriṇaḥ

rājānaḥ kṣatriyā vaiśyā amātyā mantriṇo janāḥ|śilpino vaṇijaḥ sārthavāhāścāpi mahājanāḥ

paurā jānapadā grāmyastathānyadeśavāsinaḥ|evamanyepi lokāśca saddharmaguṇavāñchinaḥ

sarve tatra mahotsāhaistena sārddha mudā carat|evaṃ sa ratnapāṇistaiḥ sarvaiḥ lokai samanvitaḥ

pūjāṅgāni samādāya mahotsāhai mudācarat|evaṃ sa pravarañchrīmān sarvāṃllokān vinodayan

sahasā tatra prāgatya dadarśemaṃ jinālayaṃ|dṛṣṭvainaṃ sa mahāsattvaḥ muditaḥ samupāsaran

yathāvidhi samabhyarcya praṇatvā prābhajanmudā|evaṃ sarve'pi te lokāḥ sahāyāstasyamaharṣayaḥ

yathāvidhi samabhyarcya mahotsāhai mudābhajan|evaṃ te sakalā lokā stutvā japtvā ca dhāraṇī

pradakṣiṇāni kṛtvoṣṭa praṇatvā prābhajan mudāḥ|evamanye mahāsattvā bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ

daśadigbhyaḥ samāgatya prābhajannimamīśvaraṃ|ahamapi tadā tena sahetya ratnapāṇinā

śraddhayā samupāśritya prābhajanimamīśvaraṃ|etatpuṇyānubhāvena bodhiṃ prāpya kalāvapi

jitvā māragaṇān sarvān dharmādhipo bhavāmyahaṃ||ye yesya samupāśritya bhajeyuḥ śraddhayā mudā

te te sarve'pi saṃbodhiṃ prāpya syuḥ sugatā drutaṃ||evaṃ mahattaraṃ puṇyasya sevāsamudbhavaṃ

bhadraśrīsadguṇāpannaṃ sambodhijñānasādhanaṃ|iti sarvai munīndraiśca samākhyātaṃ samantataḥ

yūyamapi parijñāya bhajatāsya samādarāt|munīndrā api sarve'sya catuḥsandhyaṃ divāniśaṃ

dṛṣṭvā dhyātvā smṛtiṃ dhṛtvā prābhajantaṃ samādarāt|bhajanti sāmprataṃ sarve buddhāḥ sarve digāśritāḥ

anāgatāśca sarve'pi bhajiṣyanti tathā sadā|asya darśanamātreṇa praduṣṭā api pāpinaḥ

nirmukta pātakā hyāśu bhaveyu nirmalendriyāḥ|tataste nirmalātmāno niḥkleśo brahmacāriṇaḥ

durgati naiva gaccheyuḥ kutrāpi hi kadācana||saṅgatāveva saṃjātā bhadraśrīsadguṇāśrayāḥ

bodhisattvā mahāsattvā bhaveyuḥ sugatātmajāḥ|tataste bodhisambhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya nirvṛtiṃ padamāpnuyuḥ|iti natvā sadāpyasya kṛtvā darśanamādarāt

anumodyābhyanusmṛtvā dhyātvānubhāvya sarvadā|nāmāpi ca samuccārya śraddhayā bodhivāchibhiḥ

yathāśakti prakarttavyā bhaktisevā sadādarāt|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa śrīghanena niśamyate

maitreyapramukhāḥ sarve sabhālokāḥ prabodhitāḥ|tathetyabhyanumodanto dharmmadhāto svayambhuvaḥ

śraddhayā samupāśritya saṃbhajituṃ samīcchire|atha sarvve sabhālokā dharmmadhātoḥ svayambhuvaḥ

pūjāphalaviśeṣāṇi saṃśrotuṃ punarīcchireḥ|tanmatvā sa mahāsattvo maitreyaḥ sugatātmaja

bhagavantaṃ munīndrantaṃ prārthayadevamānataḥ|bhagavannasya pūjāyā viśeṣaphalavistaraṃ

sarvve ime sabhālokāḥ śrotumicchanti sāmprataṃ|tadbhagavān samupādiśya pūjāphalaviśeṣatāṃ

imāṃ sarvān sabhāsīnāṃ saṃbodhayitumarhati|iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena maitreyeṇa niśamya saḥ

bhagavānstān sabhālokān sampaśyanaivamādiśat|śṛṇudhvaṃ sakalā lokā asya pūjāphalaṃ mahat

viśeṣeṇa pravakṣyāmi sarve lokābhibodhane|pañcāmṛtaiḥ sahāmbhobhiḥ saṃśobhitasunirmmalaiḥ

ye mudā snāpayantimaṃ dharmmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|mandākinyāṃ sadā snātvā te viśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ

divyasukhāni bhuṃjāno prānte yāni jinālayaṃ|saurabhya dravyasaṃyukti yatra caityai svayambhuvi

modayanto jagaccittaṃ dhūpayanti mudā sadāḥ|te sugaṃdhitasaumyāṅgā mānyā devā surairapi

śrīmantaḥ susvasaṃpannā bhavanti ratnasannibhāḥ|pañcagandhairmudā yatra dharmmadhāto jinālaye

liptārādhya samāśritya prabhajanti sadādarāt|saptaratnasametāste bhadraśrīsadguṇāśrayāḥ

sarvve lokā hitodyuktā bhavanti kṣitipādhipāḥ|ye cātra munirājendrā vicitra cīvarāmbaraiḥ

pravārya śraddhayā bhaktyā saṃbhajante samādarāt||te divyadṛṣya kauśeya ratnābharaṇabhūṣitāḥ

sujñā dharmādhipāḥ santo bhavanti bhadracāriṇaḥ|ye caimaṃ kusumaiḥ sarvvai jalajai sthalajairapi

arccayitvā samāśritya saṃbhajante pramoditāḥ|mahīndraśrīsamṛddhāste śakrādhikaprabhānvitāḥ

bhadraśrīsukhasaṃpannā bhavanti bodhibhāginaḥ|yacainaṃ puṣpamālābhi racitābhirmanoharaiḥ

sarvvapuṣpaiḥ pralambābhiḥ śobhayitvā bhajanti ca|bhavanti śrīsamṛddhāste dharmmakāmāḥ suradhipāḥ

satkīrttirguṇasaṃraktāḥ śubhagā bodhicāriṇaḥ|ye ca puṣpāṇi sarvāṇi mudātra sugatālaye

avakīrya samārādhya bhajanti śaraṇāśritāḥ|te'pi devādhipā svarge gatā mahyāṃ nṛpādhipāḥ

mahacchrīguṇasampannā bhavanti bodhibhāginaḥ|dhṛtasugandhitailādisaṃpradīptāṃ tamopahāṃ

jvālayanti mudā ye'smiṃ dharmmadhātau jinālaye|suduṣṭayaḥ surupāste jñānadīpatamopahāḥ

bhūpārcitapadāmbhojā bhavanti bodhibhāginaḥ|praṇītaṃ surasaṃbhojyaṃ valbhagandhasamanvitaṃ

ye cāsminnupaḍhaukitvā prabhajanti samādarāt|ṛddhimanto nṛpeṃdrāste saptaratnasamanvitā

svargge devādhipāścāpi bhavanti bodhibhāginaḥ|ye cāsmiṃ surasaṃpānaṃ suvarṇṇagandhasaṃyutaṃ

upaḍhaukya samārādhya prabhajante samāhitāḥ|te valiṣṭhā mahīpendrāḥ śrīsamṛddhā nirogiṇaḥ

svarge gatāśca devendrā bhavanti bodhibhāginaḥ|ye cāsmiṃ skandamūlāni bījapatraphalāni ca

śraddhayā samupasthāpya saṃbhajante samāśritāḥ|te prabhuktvā yathākāmaṃ bhogyāni vividhānyapi

saddharmmasādhanā raktāḥ saṃyāntyante jinālayaṃ|ye cāsmiṃ sugatādhāre pathyauṣadhagaṇānyapi

samarpya śraddhayā nityaṃ prasevyante samādarāt|te valiṣṭhā supuṣṭāṅgāḥ saumyendriyā nirāmayāḥ

rājyaśrīsukhamābhujya saṃyātyante sukhāvatī|ye cāpyatra jinādhāre dharmmadhātau svayambhuvi

vitatyoccai vitānaṃ ca sasevyante samādarāt||dhanyāste guṇino vaṃdyāḥ śuddhavaṃśā vicakṣaṇāḥ

sarvvārthasiddhisampannāḥ prayāntyante jinālayaṃ|ye cāsmin sugatāvāse vicitrānucchritāndhvajān

avaropya mahotsāhaiḥ saṃbhajante'bhinanditā|śrīsadguṇasukhadhārā bhūtvā bhūpādhipā bhuvi

svarge devādhipāścānte saṃprayānti jinālayaṃ|sauvarṇṇaratnapuṣpādi chatrāṇi vividhāni ye

āropyatra mahotsāhaiḥ saṃbhajante pramoditāḥ|te narendrāḥ surendrāśca chatropamāḥ sadā bhave

mahatsukhāni bhuktvānte saṃprayānti jinālayaṃ|ye cāsmiṃ sugatāvāse patākāḥ pañcaraṅgikāḥ

samāvalaṃvayitvāpi saṃbhadrante mahotsavaiḥ|te bhūtvātra mahīpālāḥ sadā devādhipā api

bhadraśrīsatsukhaṃ bhuktvā saṃyātyante jinālayaṃ|ye cāsmin vividhairvādyaiḥ saṃgītimurujādibhiḥ

tauryai vaṃśādibhiścāpi saṃsevyante mahotsavaiḥ|te manojñasvanādivyaśrīśrīśrīsadguṇāśrayāḥ

saddharmmasādhanaṃ kṛtvā vrajanti sugatālayaṃ|salājākṣatapuṣpāṇi ye cāsmiṃ sugatālaye

prakṣipya śraddhayā bhaktyā saṃbhajante samādarāt|durgatinte na gacchanti saṃjātāḥ saṅgatau
sadā

sarvasattvahitaṃ kṛtvā saṃprayānti jinālayaṃ|sadhātudravyaratnādi dakṣiṇānyatra ye mudā

śraddhayā pariḍhaukitvā saṃbhajante sadādaraṃ|divyaśrīsukhabhuṃjānā bhadraśrī sadguṇāśrayāḥ

sarvasattvahitaṃ kṛtvā saṃprayānti jinālaye|ye cāpi stutibhistathyairenaṃ buddhālayaṃ mudā||153|padyairgadyamayaiścāpi
stutvā bhajanti sādaraṃ|bahuratnasamṛddhāste sarvvavidyāvicakṣaṇāḥ

bhūpāḥ svargādhipāścāpi bhūtvānte yānti saugataṃ|śraddhayainaṃ jagannāthaṃ samāśrayaṃ svayambhūvaṃ

natvāṣṭāṃgaiḥ prasannā ye saṃbhajante samādaraṃ|saptaratnasametāste nṛpādhipā mahardhikāḥ

saddharmmasādhanāraktā bhavanti bodhicāriṇaḥ|ye cainaṃ caityarājendramanekaśaḥ pradakṣiṇāṃ

kṛtvā dhyātvāpyanusmṛtvā nāmoccārya bhajantyapi|jātismarāścirāyuṣkā matimantaḥ suvarṇṇinaḥ

vandyāḥ pūjyāśca mānyāste bhaveyurbodhicāriṇaḥ|śuddhāśca bhasmasaṃkṣālaiḥ samālipya samantataḥ

samabhyarcyamahotsāhai ye bhajantyenamīśvaraṃ|śokakleśāgnisaṃtāpavivarjitāścirāyuṣaḥ

nīrogāḥ sukhino devā bhaveyu bhūmipāśca te|ye ca nirmālyamākṛṣya śodhayitvātra sarvataḥ

śramupāśritya sevyante sambuddhabhaktimānasāḥ|nirmuktikleśaśokāste darśanīyāḥ śubhendriyāḥ

śrīmantaḥ puṇḍarīkāsyā bhaveyurbodhicāriṇaḥ|jīrṇe śīrṇe vibhagne'smin pratisaṃskṛtya ye mudā

pratiṣṭhāpya mahotsāhaiḥ saṃbhajante samādarāt|sarvasampatsamṛddhāste puṣṭendriyā nirāmayāḥ

dharmakāmāḥ śubhācārā bhaveyurbodhicāriṇaḥ|jalpitvā yasya mantrāṇi dhāraṇīśca samāhitāḥ

dhyātvā smṛtvā samuccārya nāmāpi prabhajanti ca|te'pi sarve mahāsattvāḥ pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ

bhadraśrīsadguṇādhārā bhaveyurbodhicāriṇaḥ|evaṃ mahattaraṃ puṇyamasmiṃ dharmmādhipālaye

śraddhābhajanasaṃbhūtamityādiṣṭaṃ munīśvaraiḥ|mayaitatpuṇyasaṃkṣiptamātraṃ tu kathyate'dhunā

samagraṃ vistarenātra samākhyātuṃ na śakyate|evaṃ matvāsya satkārapūjāphalaṃ mahattaraṃ

śraddhayā śaraṇaṃ gatvā kartavyaṃ bhajanaṃ sadā|yepyasmiṃñcharaṇaṃ gatvā śraddhayā samupāśritāḥ

suprasannāśayā bhaktyā bhajanti bodhimānasāḥ|na te gacchanti kutrāpi durgatiṃ ca kadācana

saṃjātāḥ sadgatiṣveva bhaveyurbodhicāriṇaḥ|sadā te sukule jātā bodhisattvā vicakṣaṇāḥ

sarvasattva hitā dhānaṃ careyurvratamābhavaṃ|tataste bodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya nirvṛtipadamāpnuyuḥ|na hicitta prasādaspa svalpābhavati dakṣiṇā

tathāgateṣu saddharmma saṃbuddha śrāvakeṣvapi||evaṃ hyacintyāḥ saṃbuddhāḥ buddha dharmāśca nirmalāḥ

acintyohi prasannānāṃ vipākaśca mahāphala|evaṃ matvā triratneṣu bhaktipūjā phalaṃ mahat

kāryābhaktiṣu sadātraiva dharmmadhātau jinālaye|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndrena śrutvā sarve samāśritāḥ

lokāstatheti vijñapya prātyananda pravodhitāḥ|iti me guruṇādiṣṭaṃ śrutamayā tathocyate

tvamapyevaṃ sadā rājan bhaja tatra jinālaye|tatpuṇyena te bhadraṃ nirutpātaṃ sadābhavet

bodhicitta rasaṃ ca saṃprāpya bodhisattvo bhaverapi|tataḥ sambodhi saṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā tathākramaṃ

mārānnirjjitya saṃbodhi prāpya buddhapadaṃ labheḥ|iti śāstārhatā diṣṭaṃ samyakanarādhipaḥ

aśokaḥ sasabhālokaḥ prābhyanandaṃ prabodhitaḥ|iti śrī svayambhūtpatti kathāyā śrī svayambhū
bhaṭṭārakoddeśa pūjāvarṇanā nāma dvītīyo'dhyāya samāpta|
