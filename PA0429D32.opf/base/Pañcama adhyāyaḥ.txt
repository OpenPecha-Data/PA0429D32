sarva sattvāhitādhānaṃ saṃcaradhva jagaddhite|tataḥ saṃbodhiṃ saṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

āśu tribodhimāsādya prāpsyatha saugataṃ padaṃ|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa śrutvā sarve pravodhitāḥ

maitreyādi sabhālokāḥ prāpyananda pravodhitāḥ|iti śrī svayaṃbhū samutpatti kathāyāṃ aneka tīrtha
saṃjāta puṇya mahātmya varṇano nāma pañcamo'dhyāya|
