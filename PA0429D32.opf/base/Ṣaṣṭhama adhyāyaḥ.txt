tataste mānavāḥ sarve ṛṣi vipranṛpādayaḥ|taṃ munīndraṃ samabhyarcya saṃpraṇatvā yathākramaṃ

tatsabhāṃ samupāśritya parivṛtya samantataḥ|puraskṛtya samudvīkṣya saṃtasthire samāhitāḥ

tatsaddharmmāmṛtaṃ pītvā sarve lokāḥ pravodhitāḥ|saddharmma sādhanā yuktā babhūvu rbodhimānasāḥ

tadā vikramaśīlākhye vihāre bhikṣurātmavit|sasaṃghā vyaharad dharmmaśrīmitrākhyaḥ sudhīryatiḥ

tataḥ sa satmatiḥ sarvān saṃghā nāma prasādaraṃ|sabhāmadhyāsanāsīnastasthau dhyānasamāhitaḥ

taṃ sabhāsana āsīnaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarve'pi sāṃghikāḥ|tatsaddharmmāmṛtaṃ pātumicchataḥ samupāgatāḥ

tatra ye yatiṃ natvā parivṛtya samantataḥ|puraskṛtya samīkṣanta upatasthuḥ samāhitāḥ

tataḥ sa yatirālokya sarvānstānsamupāśritān|mañjuśrīnāma saṃgītiṃ samākhyātaṃ yathātkramāt

tatsamādiṣṭamākarṇyaṃ sarve lokāḥ sabhāśritāḥ|saṃbuddha guṇamahātmyaṃ matvānandapravodhitāḥ

tataḥ sarve'pi lokāste brāhmaṇa bhūmipādayaḥ|natvā taṃ yatimāmantrya svasvālayaṃ mudāyayuḥ

tataste śrāvakā vijñā yatayo brahmacāriṇaḥ|dvādaśākṣara guhyārthaṃ samyak śrotuṃmamīcchire

tataste yoginaḥ sarve kṛtāñjali puṭā mudā|śāstāraṃ taṃ yatiṃ natvā samāmatryaivamavruvan

bhadanta dvādaśānāṃ yadakṣarāṇāṃ viśeṣataḥ|viśuddhiṃ śrotumicchāmastatsamādeṣṭumarhati

iti tai rprārthitaṃ dharmmaśrī mitraḥ sasudhīrapi|dvādaśākṣara guhyārthaṃ viśuddhiṃ na samādiśat

taṃ viśuddhā nabhijñātā viṣaṇṇātmā sa utthitaḥ|dhyānāgāraṃ samāsīno dhyātvaivaṃ samacintayan

naitadakṣaraṃ guhyārthaṃ viśuddhirjñāyate mayā|tatkathaṃ upadekṣyāmi hāhā kutra bhrameyahi

iti cintā viṣarṇṇo'tmā lajjā saṃmohitāśayaḥ|smṛtvā ratnatrayaṃ dhyātvā tasthau dhairyasamāhitaḥ

tatkṣaṇe sa triratnānāṃ smṛti puṇyānubhāvataḥ|evaṃ matiṃ mahāvīrya mahotsāhinimāptavān

tatastatmati śauṇḍau'sauḥ puna dhyātvā samāhitaḥ|manasā sarva lokeṣu vicārayan vyalokayan

tadāpaśyanmahācīne uttarasyāṃ nagottame|mañjuśrīyaṃ mahābhijñaṃ sarva vidyādhipeśvaraṃ

bodhisattvaṃ mahāsattvaṃ sarva dharmmādhipaprabhuṃ|sarva guhya viśuddhārtha vijñānajñānadāyakaṃ

taṃ paśyan manasā dharmmaśrī mitraḥ samutsukaḥ|sahasotthāya tānsaṃghān sabhāmantryaivamavravīt

bho bhadanto gamiṣyāmi mahācīne nagottame|mañjuśrīyaṃ mahāsattvaṃ draṣṭumicchāmi sāṃprataṃ

etasyā nāmasaṃgītyā guhya viśuddhi vistaraṃ|pṛṣṭā samyak vijñāya āgamiṣyāmyahaṃ drutaṃ

yāvannāhamihāyāta stāvatsarve samāhitāḥ|triratna bhajanaṃ kṛtvā tiṣṭhata mā viṣīdata

ityuktvā sa mahābhijñastataḥ saṃprasthito drutaṃ|sañcarannatra nepālaviṣaye samupāyayau

tamāyātaṃ yatiṃ natvā mañjudevaḥ sa sarvavit|svāntike samupādraṣṭumecchat ṛddhiṃ pradarśayan

tataḥ maṃjudevo'pi bhūtvā kṛṣikaraḥ svayaṃ|śārdūla mṛgarājābhyāṃ halenākarṣayan mahīṃ

taṃ dṛṣṭvā dūrato dharmmaśrīmitro ativismitaḥ|kimetat mahadāścaryamitidraṣṭumuyācarat

tataḥ samupāśritya dṛṣṭvā tanmahadadbhutaṃ|kṛṣikaraṃ tamāmantryapapracchaiva vyavalokayan

bho sādho ito deśānmahācīna nagoktamaḥ|pañcaśīrṣaḥ kiyaddūre tadupadeṣṭumarhati

iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena śrutvā sa halavāhakaḥ|suciraṃ taṃ yatiṃ paśyansādaramevamavravīt

yattvaṃ kuta ihāyāsi kimarthamuktarāpathe|mahācīnasya dūrato gantuṃ tvaṃ paripṛccha se

adya pravartate sāyaṃ tad vihāre mamāśrame|uṣitvā prāta rutthāya gaccha maddeśitātpathaḥ

iti tenoditaṃ dharmaśrī mitro niśaṃmya saḥ|tathetyanumataṃ dhṛtvā tūṣṇīṃ bhūtvā vyatiṣṭhataḥ

tataḥ saṃbodhitaṃ bhikṣu matvā sa halavāhakaḥ|tau śārdula mṛgendro dvautatraivāntavyardhāpayet

halaṃ tu sarvalokānāṃ saṃpravodhana hetunā|tatraivocca sthala kṣetre yūpa vadavaropayat

adyāpi tatmahīsthānaṃ mañjuśrībhūḥ prasidhyate|sāvāceti prasiddhā ca yatrāvaropitaṃ halaṃ

tatastaṃ yatimāhuya sāyaṃ sa halavāhakaḥ|tatra praviśya dharmaśrīmitra saṃprati vismitaḥ

tato mūlaphala skandha patrādi bhogamādarāt|datvā tasmai svayaṃ bhuktvā tasthau sa halavāhakaḥ

tataḥ sa vividhāṃ dharmmaśrīmitrorajanī kathāṃ|bhāṣitvā taṃ mahābhijñaṃ kṛṣikaraṃ vyanodayan

tataḥ sa mañjudevastaṃ yati chātrālaye niśi|preṣayitvā svayaṃ gahvāgāre śayyāṃ samāśrayat

tatra praviśya dharmmaśrī mitra saṃprati vismitaḥ|suptvākṣaṇaṃ samutthāya manasaivaṃ vyacintayat

nādyātra śayanīyaṃ yadayaṃpumānmaharddhikaḥ|bhāryāyā sahasākathyaṃ kiṃkiṃ kuryād vinodayaṃ

iti dhyātvā sadharmmaśrīmitra yatirutthitaḥ|saṃvara nibhṛtaṃ tasya dvāramūlamupāśrayet

tatkṣaṇe mañjudevaṃ taṃ keśinī supriyā satī|śayāsanasamāsīnā bharktāramevamavravīt

svāminko'yaṃ yati rdhīmānkimarthamasmadāśrame|iha kutaḥ samāyāta statsamādeṣdumarhati

iti saṃprārthitaṃ devyā mañjudevo niśamya saḥ|keśinīṃ tāṃ priyāṃ bhāryāṃ sampaśyannevamavravīt

sādhu śṛṇu priye devī yadartha yamimihāgataḥ|tadartha hi mahaddhetuṃ vakṣyāmi te vicārayat

ayaṃ bhikṣu mahābhijño bodhisattvo mahāmatiḥ|vikhyāto yo mahādharmmaśrīmitrobhidho yati

vikramaśīla ākhyāte vihāre sa nivāsakaḥ|nāmasaṃgīti vyākhyānaṃ śikṣebhyo vistaraṃ vyadhāt

dvādaśākṣara guhyārtha viśuddhi jñāna vistaraṃ|nāma samyagupākhyātuṃ śaknoti na sudhīrapi

tadāyaṃ ca viṣaṇṇātmāḥ dhyānāgāre samāśritaḥ|dhyātvā lokeṣu sarvatra vilokyaivaṃ vyacintayan

iti saṃprāthitaṃ devyā mañjudevo niśamya saḥ|keśinīṃ tāṃ priyāṃ bhāryā sampaśyannevamavravīt

sādhu devī tava prītyā sāmpratamupadiśyate|etadartha mahāguhyaṃ gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ

ityuktvā mañjudevo'sau tasyai devyai yathāvidhiḥ|dvādaśākṣara guhyārthaṃ viśuddhi samupādiśat

etatsarva samākhyātaṃ vistaraṃ sa mahāmatiḥ|dharmaśrīmitra ākarṇya prātyananda pravodhitaḥ

tata samudito dharmmaśrī mitra utthite mudā|mañjuśrīrayameveti niścayaṃ samupāyayau

tataḥ sasuprasannātmā dvāramūle kṛtāñjaliḥ|aṣṭāṃga praṇatiṃ kṛtvā tasthau tadgata mānasaḥ

tataḥ prātaḥ samutthāya keśinī mokṣadāyanī|dvārakapāramudadhāṭayaḥ vahirgantumupākramat

tatra taṃ yatimālokya dvāramūle vyavasthitaṃ|bhītā sā keśinī devī drutamupācaratprabhoḥ

tāṃ pratyāgatāṃ dṛṣṭvā mañjudevaḥ sa sarvavit|vibhinnāsyāṃ samālokya papracchaivaṃ adhīravat

devī kiṃdvāramudhyāṭya sattvaraṃ tvamupāgatāḥ|dṛṣṭvā kiṃ tatra bhītāsi tatsatyaṃ vada me puraḥ

iti pṛṣṭe jagacchāstā bharktā sā keśinī virāt|svāminaṃ taṃ samālokya śanairevaṃnyavedayet

svāminyati namaskṛtvā dvāramūle nipātitaḥ|jīvito vā mṛtau vāsau mayā na jñāyate khalu

ityuktaṃ bhāryayā śrutvā mañjudevaḥ sa utthitaḥ|upetya dvāramūle taṃ saṃdadarśa nipātitaṃ

dṛṣṭvā sa mañjudevastaṃ yatiṃ dhyātvā jitendriyaṃ|hastaṃ dhṛtvā samutthāya saṃpaśyannevavravīt

yate kimarthamatraivaṃ dvāraṃ dhyātvāvatiṣṭhasi|tatmamapurataḥ sabhyaṃ vadaya te samāhitaṃ

bhagavannātha sarvajña sarvavidyādhipa prabho|bhavatpādāmbuje bhaktyā śaraṇe samupāśraye

iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena mañjudeva niśamya sa|vijñāya taṃ mahābhijñaṃ saṃpaśyannevamavravīt

kathaṃ vinā abhiṣekaṃ te mantrārthamupadeśyate

tāvadatrabhiṣeka tvaṃ gṛhvāṇhedaṃ yadīcchasi|ityuktaṃ mañjudevena niśamya sa yatiḥ sudhīḥ

mañjudevaṃ namostasya sāñjalirevamavravīt|sarvajña bhagavāñchāstā nirdhano'hamakiṃcanaḥ

kiṃ dāsye bhavataṃ śāstre dakṣiṇāṃ guru bhaktimān|iti tenoditaṃ śrutvāḥ mañjudevaḥ sasanmatiḥ

saṃpaśyansteyatī dharmaśrīmitramevamavravīt|yataḥ kiṃ dhanasaṃpatyā śraddhā te yadi vidyate

tuṣyante guruvo bhaktimātreṇātra dhanenatu|ityuktvā mañjudevena dharmaśrīmitra unmatāḥ

aṣṭāṃgestaṃ guruṃ natvā prārthayadevamādarāt|bhagavanyadi bhaktyaiva tuṣyate'tra bhavān mama

bhavatāṃ suśrūṣāmeṣa karomi bhaktimānahaṃ|iti me kṛpayā śāsta abhiṣeka yathāvidhiḥ

datvā dvādaśa mantrārthaṃ viśuddhi dātumarhasi|iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena mañjudevaṃ sa sarvavit

bhaktimanta tamālokya paṇyamevamabhāṣata|dāsyāmi te mahābhāga bhakti śraddhāsti te yadi

abhiṣeka prasannātmā samādatsva samāhitaḥ|tataḥsa mañjudevaḥ śrī vajrācārya yathāvidhi

saṃsthāpya maṇḍalaṃ dharmadhātuvāgīśvarābhidhaṃ|tanmaṇḍalaṃ samārādhya samabhyarcya yathāvidhiṃ

abhiṣekaṃ prasannāya tasmai dadau sa vajradhṛk|tatastān maṇḍale devān saṃdarśayaṃ yathākramāt

pūjayitvā yathāśakti śaraṇe samayojayet|tatastasmai prasannāya mañjudevo yathāvidhi

dvādaśākṣara guhyārtha viśuddhisamupādiśat|tato labdhābhiṣeko'sau dharmaśrīmitra utmanā

dvādaśabhūmi guhyārtha viśuddhijñānamāptavān|tasmai śāstre sabhāryāya svamātmānaṃ sa dakṣiṇāṃ

saṃkalpya śraddhayā bhaktyā natvaivaṃ prārthayan mudā|bhagavan nātha sarvajña bhavat kṛpā prasādataḥ

saṃprāptapūrṇa saṃkalpo bhavāmi śrīguṇārthabhṛt|tat sadāhaṃ bhavatpādaśaraṇe samupāśritaḥ

yathātra bhavādiṣṭaṃ tathaiva saṃcare bhave|tat me'nujñādi datvātra saṃbodhijñānasādhanaṃ

sarvasattva hitārthena careyaṃ bodhisaṃvaraṃ|iti saṃprārthya dharmaśrī mitra sa samupāśritaḥ

śraddhābhakti prasannātmā gurusevā paro'bhavat|tataḥ sa mañjudevastaṃ mahāsattvaṃ vicakṣaṇaṃ

matvā saṃbodhi caryāyāṃ niyoktuṃ saṃvyanodayan|sādhu sādhu mahābhāgaḥ saṃcaratvaṃ jagaddhite

saṃbodhi sādhanaṃ bodhicaryā vrata sadā bhava|etatpuṇyābhi liptātmā pariśuddha trimaṇḍalāḥ

bodhisattvā mahābhijñā bhaveḥ śrī sadguṇāśrayāḥ|tatastvaṃ bodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

niḥkleśo bodhiprāpto'haṃ sambuddhapadamāpnuyāḥ|iti me śāsanaṃ dhṛtvā smṛtvā dhyātvā samāhitaḥ

triratnaṃ samupāśritya saṃcarasva jagaddhite|yadi jagaddhitaṃ kartuṃ saṃbodhiṃ prāpnumicchasi

saddharmaṃ samupādiśya sarvān lokān pravodhaya||tatastān bodhitān sarvān kamena bodhisādhanaṃ

bodhimārge pratiṣṭhāpya cārayasva jagaddhite|tatasteṣāṃ samālokya cittaṃ saṃbodhi niścitaṃ

triyānaṃ samupādiśya paramārthe niyojaye|evaṃ kṛtvā mahaddharmamāśu sambodhisādhanaṃ

bhadraśrī sadguṇāpannāṃ samāpnuyā jagacchubhe|tenāśuḥ pariśuddhātmā sambuddhapadamāptavān

jagaddharmmamayaṃ kṛtvā jinālayaṃ samāpnuyāḥ|iti bhadravrataṃ dhṛtvā gatvā tvaṃ svāśrame punaḥ

vyākhyāya nāma saṃgītiṃ saddharmmaṃ saṃpracāraye|ahamapi mahāsattva śrotuṃ tvad dharmmadeśanāṃ

sāṃghikāṃścāpi tāndruṣṭumāyāsyāmi tavāśrame|iti śāstā samādiṣṭaṃ niśaṃmya sa mahāmatiḥ

dharmaśrī mitra ālokyaḥ taṃ gurumevamavravīt|maharddhiko bhagavāñchāsta vijñāsya te kathaṃ mayā

iti cihnaṃ samādhāya tatrāgantuṃ samarhati|iti tenoditaṃ śrutvā mañjudevaḥ sa sanmati

dharmaśrī mitramālokyaḥ punarevamabhāṣata|vatsāhamutpalaṃ dhṛtvā samāyāsyāmi te sabhāṃ

tena cihnena māṃ yāta saṃjānīṣva samāgataṃ|iti satyaṃ samādhāya dhṛtvānuśāsanaṃ mama

saddharmma samupādeṣṭuṃ pravāhi testu maṃgalaṃ|iti śāstā samādiṣṭaṃ śrutvā sa yatirutsukaḥ

śāstāraṃ taṃ samālokya praṇatvaivamabhāṣataḥ|prasīdatu bhagavāñchāstāḥ kṣantumarhati cāgasaṃ

bhavatprasādataḥ sarve sidhyate me samīhitaṃ|tadanuśāsanaṃ dhṛtvā bhavatāṃ tatra jagaddhite

vyākhyātuṃ nāmasaṃgīti saṃcare sāmprataṃ guroḥ|iti saṃprārthya dharmmaśrī mitra saḥ saṃprāsāditaḥ

śāstustasya padāmbhojaṃ natvā saṃprasthito tataḥ|mātrorācārya yāścāpi mokṣadā varadākhyayoḥ

pādāmvujeṣu saṃnatvā saṃprasthitaḥ pramoditaḥ|tataḥ sa sanmati dharmmaśrīmitraḥ sahasāvrajan

āśu svāśramamāsādya vihāraṃ samupāviśat|tatra taṃ yatimāyātaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarve'pi sāṃghikāḥ

praṇatvā kuśalaṃ pṛṣṭavā praveśayan nijālaye|tataḥ paredyurāmantraya sarvānsaṃghānsa sanmatiḥ

vyākhyātuṃ nāmasaṃgīti sabhāsanaṃ samāśrayat|taṃ sabhāsanamāsīnaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarve'pi sāṃdhikāḥ

divja bhūpādayaścāpi sarve lokāḥ samāgatāḥ|tatra sarve'pi te lokāḥ praṇatvā taṃ yati kramāt

parivṛtya puraskṛtya samantata upāśrayat|tān sarvānsamupāsīnān dṛṣṭvā sa yatirātmavit

vyākhyāya nāmasaṃgītiṃ sa viśuddhi samādiśat|tadā tatra manastasya jijñāsituṃ sa mañjuvāk

dhṛtvotpalaṃ vinidyāṃgaḥ kucīvaramupācarat|tatra sa makṣikān kāye utpalena nivārayan

samāgatya sabhaikānte paśyallokānupāśrayet|samāśritaṃ sa dharmmaśrīmitro dṛṣṭvātmanā guruṃ

utpalena parijñāya manasaivaṃ vyacintayat|aho nūnamayaṃ śāstā mano jijñāsituṃ mama

kucarā durbhagākāro dhṛtvotpalamihāgataḥ|tatkathamahamutthāya pratyuṅgamya na meya hi

atha paśyannamaskāraṃ nakuryāḥ gurave kathaṃ|yadyatrāhaṃ samutthāya na meyamenamādarāt

dṛṣṭvā lokā ime māṃ dhikkuryuḥ sarve vicārataḥ|ete na jñāyate yaṃ hi mañjuśrī ṛddhimānapi

īdṛgevāsya śāstā yaṃ miti me syād vihāsyatāṃ|iti dhyātvā sa dharmmaśrīmitro lajjābhimohitaḥ

manasaivaṃ namaskṛtvā śāstārantamamānayet|tataḥ sa vimukhī bhūya paśyannapya vibhāvitaḥ

ajñāta vānnivāpaśyan padamātramupādiśat|tataḥ sarve'pi te lokāḥ śrutvā tatsaddharmmadeśanāṃ

utthāya taṃ yatiṃ natvā svasvālayamupācaran|tata loke gate dharmmaśrī mitraḥ sa samutthitaḥ

śāstāraṃ taṃ samālokya vandituṃ samupācarat|taṃ dṛṣṭvā mañjudevo'sau vandituṃ samupāgataṃ

apaśyan vimukhī bhūya tataḥ saṃprasthito'carat|taṃ dṛṣṭvā vimukhī bhūta dharmmaśrī mitra ātmanaḥ

aparādha mahatpāpamanusmṛtvāpatadbhuvi|nipatantaṃ tamālokya mañjuśrīḥ sa kṛpānidhiḥ

sahasā pāṇinā dhṛtvā samutthāya tathācarat|tatra sa utthito dharmmaśrī mitrastaṃ mahāmatiṃ

pracarantaṃ samālokya natvāhaivaṃ mṛṣā punaḥ|bhagavanna mayā dṛṣṭo bhavānatra samāgataḥ||172paścādutpalacihnena
jñāyate'tra samāśritaḥ|ityuktvā sa mṛṣāvādaṃ sāñjalistasya saṅguroḥ

mañjudevasya pādābje praṇanāma rudan punaḥ|tatra tasya mṛṣā vaktuṃ rubhe'pi nayane mukhāt

śāstuḥ pādābjayo ragre nipetaṃtu mahītale|tatpatita sa dharmmaśrīmitro vihatamānasaḥ

cirādrudaṃ samutthāyaḥ śāstāramevamavravīt|bhagavan yatmayājñānādaparādhaṃ kṛtaṃ gurau

bhavati tad bhavāñchāstā kṣantuṃmarhati durmateḥ|iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena mañjuśrīḥ sa kṛpānidhiḥ

vicakṣuṣkaṃ tamālokyaḥ kṛpādṛśaivamavravīt|yadabhilajjayā karmma jñātvāpi duṣkṛtaṃ tvayā

yathā

jñānaṃ hi te'sti yattena jñānaśrī mitra ucyase|ityuktyaiva sa mañjuśrīḥ kṣaṇādantarhita stataḥ

ākāśāt pakṣivad gatvā svāśrame samupāyayau|atraitatsarvavṛttāntaṃ bhāryayoḥ puratormudā

samākhyāya sa mañjuśrī stasthau lokahitārthabhṛt|tataḥ prabhṛtisa jñānaśrīmitro jñānacakṣuṣā

paśyan saddharmmamākhyāya prācaracca jagaddhite|tadā maitreya tenāsya dharmadhātoḥ svayaṃbhuvaḥ

abhutprasiddhitaṃ dharmadhātuṃ vāgīśvarābhidhaṃ|iti matvātra ye dharmadhātu vāgīśvara narāḥ

śraddhayā vidhinābhyarcya bhajanti śaraṇāśritāḥ|abhiṣekaṃ ca saṃprāpya bodhicittā samāhitāḥ

saddharmma dhāraṇī vidyāmantrāṇi dhārayanti ye|te sarve vimalātmānaḥ pariśuddha trimaṇḍalāḥ

bodhisattvā mahāsattvāścaturbrahma vihāriṇaḥ|bhadraśrī sadguṇādhārāḥ sarva vidyā vicakṣaṇāḥ

ṛddhi siddhi mahābhijñā bhaveyurbhadracāriṇaḥ|āśuḥ sambodhi saṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

arhantastrividhāṃ bodhiṃ prāpya yāyu jiṃnālayaṃ

iti matvā'bhivāchanti prāptuṃ ye saugataṃ padaṃ|te'tra bauddhālaye dharmmadhātu vāgīśvare sadā

śraddhayā bhajanaṃ kṛtvā prāpyābhiṣekamādarāt|sadharmmadhāraṇī vidyāmantra sādhāratatparāḥ

yathāvidhi samabhyarcya saṃbodhi nihitāśrayāḥ|bodhicaryā vrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcarate jagaddhite

āśu te vimalātmānaḥ pariśuddha trimaṇḍalāḥ|bodhisattva mahāsattvaścaturbrahma vihāriṇaḥ

bhadraśrī sadguṇādhārāḥ sarvavidyā vicakṣaṇāḥ|ṛddhisiddhi mahābhijñā bhaveyu bhardracāriṇaḥ

tataḥ saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā drutaṃ kramāt|arhantamtrividhāṃ bodhiṃ prāpya yāsyatha nivṛtiṃ

ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa niśamya te sabhāśritāḥ|sarve tatheti vijñāya prābhyanandan pravodhitāḥ

itiśrī svayaṃbhū dharmmadhātu vāgīśvarābhidhāna prasiddha pravartano nāma ṣaṣṭho'dhyāyaḥ|
