ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa krakuchandena sapratuḥ|dharmmākara samākarṇya tatheti pratibudhyata

tataḥ saḥnṛpa utthāya sāñjalistaṃ munīśvaraṃ|krakucchanda sa saṃghaṃ ya praṇatvevaṃ nyavedayata

bhagavan bhavatāmājñāṃ dhṛtvāhamatra sarvadā|puraṃ vidhāya lokānāṃ hitārthe nivase khalu

tad bhavān kṛpayālokya sarvadātra himālaye|sasaṃgho dharmmamādiśya viharaṃtu jagaddhite

iti saṃprārthite tena bhagavān sa munīśvaraḥ|dharmmākaraṃ mahāsattvaṃ taṃ paśyannevamavravīt

nāhaṃ sadātra tiṣṭheyaṃ careyaṃ sarva bhūtale|sarvasattva hitārtha hi bhavāmi dharmmadikṣiṇaḥ

ityādiśya munīndreṇa krakuchandaḥ sa sāṃdhikaḥ|tataḥ saṃprasthito' nyatra deśeṃ saṃbhāṣayan
yayau

tato dharmākaraḥ so'tra vidhāya nagaraṃ tathā|rājyāṃgāni pratisthāpya rājyaṃ kṛtvādhyatiṣṭhata

tadātra sarve āgatya mandireṣu samantataḥ|āśritya saṃsthitiṃ kṛtvā nivasanto mudā caran

tato'nyepi samāyātāḥ sarvadigbhyo'tra sarvataḥ||jānapade purenekaṃ grāmeṣu nyavasan mudā

tathā devā surādyāśca sarve lokādhipā api|svasvaparijanaiḥ sārddhaṃ āgatyātra mudā vasan

tathā maharṣayaścāpi yatayo brahmacāriṇaḥ|yogino bhikṣavo'rhanto vratinaścāpyupāsakāḥ

bodhisattvā mahāsattvā ścailakāḥ śrāvakā api|yathābhilaṣite deśe kṛtvā śramaṃ samāśrayan

tathānya tīrthikāḥ śaivā vaiṣṇavāḥ kaulikā api|yathābhilaṣite sthāne kṛtvāśramaṃ samāśrayan

pratyeka sugatāścāpi samāgatya samantataḥ|vivikte āśrame ramye samāśritya mudā vasan

munīndrā api cāgatya vihṛtyātra sasāṃdhikāḥ|prārya sambodhi saddharmmaṃ samādeśyan tvarāgatāḥ

evaṃ puṇyatamā bhūmīriyaṃ himālayāhavayā|sukhāvatinibhāramyā bodhisattva samāśraya

vahūni cātra tīrthāni sarvapāpaharāṇyapi|jātāni santi sarvārtha samṛddhi siddhidāṇyapi

tadeteṣu ca tīrtheṣu snātvā carata sadvrataṃ|pāpaṃ hantuṃ śubhaṃ prāptuṃ samīchantyatra ye narāḥ

ye'tra tīrtheṣu sarveṣu snātvā nityaṃ samāhita|japa yajñādi karmmāṇi kṛtvā caranti samvaraṃ

pitṛñcāpi samabhyarcya devāñca śraddhayādarāt|datvā dānaṃ samādhāya dhyātvāpīśaṃ bhajanti ca

te'pi sarve vikalmāṣāḥ pariśuddha trimaṇḍalāḥ|śrīmantaḥ siddhi mantaśca bhaveyuḥ sadguṇāśrayāḥ

tataste sarva sattvānāṃ hitārthe dharma sādhakāḥ|bodhisattvā mahāsattvāḥ bhaveyuḥ sugatātmajāḥ

tataste bodhisambhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|triratna bhajanaṃ kṛtvā saṃcarerañjagaddhite

tataste vimalātmāno niḥkleśā vijitendriyāḥ|arhanta strividhāṃ bodhiṃ prāpyeyuḥ saugataṃ padaṃ

evaṃ matvātra tīrtheṣu sarveṣu bodhivāṃchibhiḥ|nātvā dānādi karma karttavya sarvadā bhave

ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa samādiṣṭaṃ nisamya te|sarva sabhāśritā lokāḥ prābhyanandan pravodhitāḥ

iti śrī svayaṃbhū caitya samutpatti kathā vītarāgatīrtha rāṣṭra pravarttano nāma caturtho'dhyāyaḥ
samāptaḥ|
