svayambhu purāṇaprathama adhyāyaḥsvayambhūdharmadhātusamutpatti nidānakathāom namaḥ śrī dharmadhātuvāgīśvarāya
sarva buddhadharmabodhisattvebhyaḥ|śrīmatā yena saddharmastrailoke saṃprakāśitaḥ|śrīghanaṃ
taṃ mahābuddhaṃ vandeka'haṃ śaraṇāśritaḥ

natvā trijagadīśānaṃ dharmadhātujinālayaṃ|tatsvayambhūsamuddeśaṃ vakṣāmi śṛṇutādarāt

śraddhayā yaḥ śṛṇotīmāṃ svayambhūtpattisatkathāṃ|pariśuddhatrikāyāḥ sa bodhisattvo bhaved dhruvaṃ

tadyathā bhūtpurābhijñaḥ jayaśrī sugatātmajaḥ|bodhimaṇḍavihāre sa bijahāra sasāṃdhikaḥ

tatra jineśvaro nāma bodhisattva mahāmatiḥ|śraddhayā śaraṇaṃ gatvā jayaśrīyaṃ upāśrayet

tadā dhīmānjayaśrīḥ sa sarvasattva hitārthavit|saddharmasamupādeṣṭuṃ sabhāsane samāśrayet

tatra sarve mahāsattvā bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ|arhanto bhikṣavaścāpi brahmacāriṇaḥ śrāvakāḥ

bhikṣuṇyo brahmacāriṇyo bratinaścāpyupāsakāḥ|upāsikāstathānye'pi gṛhasthaśca mahājanāḥ

brāhmaṇāstirthīkāścāpi yatayaśca tapasvinaḥ|rājāno mantriṇomātyāḥ sainyādhipatiśca paurikāḥ

grāmyā janapadāścāpi tathānyavāsino janāḥ|tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pātuṃ śraddhayā samupāgatāḥ

tatra sabhāsanāsīnaṃ tamarhataṃ jayaśrīyaṃ|abhyarcya sādaraṃ natvā tatsabhāyāṃ yathākramaṃ

kṛtāṃjalipuṭāḥ sarve parivṛtya samantataḥ|puraskṛtya samudvikṣya samāśrayan samāhitāḥ

tān sarvān samupāsīnān saddharma śravaṇotsukān|dṛṣṭvā jinaśrī bodhisattvaḥ samutthitaḥ

udvahannuttarāsaṃgaṃ sāṃjali samupāśritaḥ|jānubhyāṃ bhūtale dhṛtvā sampaśyannevamavravīt

bhadantoharṣamicchāmi carituṃ bodhisaṃvaraṃ|tadādau kiṃ vrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcareya samāhitaḥ

tad bhavān samupādisya sarvānasmān prabodhayan|bodhimārge samāyujya cārayituṃ śubherhati

iti saṃprārthitaṃ tena śrutvā ca sugatātmajaḥ|jayaśrīstaṃ mahāsattvaṃ sabhāmantraivamādiśat

śrṛṇu vatsāsti te vāṃchā sambodhisaṃvare yadi|yathākramaṃ pravakṣāmi saṃbodhivratasādhanaṃ

yo vāṃchā tatra saṃsāre carituṃ bodhisaṃvaraṃ|sa ādau śaraṇaṃ gatvā sadguruṃ samupāśrayeta

tadupadeśamāsādya yathāvidyi samāhitaḥ

tīrthe snātvā viśuddhātmā triratnaśaraṇaṃ gatāḥ|yathāvidhi samabhyarcya saṃbodhi nihitāśayaḥ

upoṣadhaṃvratamādhāya samācarejjagaddhite|evaṃ yaścarate nityaṃ saṃbodhimānasaḥ sudhīḥ

pariśuddha trikāyaḥ sa bodhisattve bhaved|bodhisattvo mahāsattvaḥ sarvvasattva hitārthabhṛt

kramātsaṃbodhi saṃbhāraṃ saṃpūrayet samāhitaḥ|etatpuṇyābhiyuktātmācaturbrahma vihāradhṛk

niḥkleśo nirjjayanmārona saṃbodhiḥ samavāpnuyāt

evaṃ sarvatra lokeṣu saddharma saṃprakāśayan|samāpya saugataṃkārya sunirvṛtimavāpnuyāt

tato'rhaṃ sugato bhūtvā sarvvānsattvānpravodhayan|bodhimārge pratiṣṭhāpya saṃvṛttau saṃpracārayet

evaṃ sarve'pi saṃbuddhāḥ yetītā apyanāgatāḥ|varttamānāśca te hayetad vratapuṇya vipākataḥ

bodhiprāpya jinā āsanbhaviṣyanti bhavantyapi|evaṃ sarve mahāsattvā bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ

arhanto'pi thā sarve pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|bodhiṃ prāpya sunirvāṇaṃ yātā yāsyanti yāntyataḥ

evaṃ yūyaṃ parijñāya yadecchatha sunivṛtiṃ|triratnaśaraṇaṃ gatvā saṃcaradhvamidaṃ vrataṃ

iti tena samādiṣṭaṃ śrutvā sa sugatātmajaḥ|jineśvaraṃ tamarhantaṃ sampaśyannevamavravīt

bhadanta śrotumichāmi tad vratasthānamuttamaṃ|etad vrataṃ caretkutra taddeśaṃ samupādiśat

iti saṃprārthite tena jayaśrīḥ sa mahāmatiḥ|jineśvaraṃ mahāsattvaṃ taṃ paśyannevamādiśat

śṛṇu vatsa samādhāyaṃ vratasthānasamuttamaṃ|munīśvarairyathākhyātaṃ tathā vakṣāmi te'dhunā

puṇyakṣetreṣu tīrtheṣu vihāre sugatāśrame||buddhānāṃ nivṛtānāṃca caityeṣu pratimāsu ca

buddhakṣetreṣu sarvatra vratasthānaṃ samuttamaṃ|eteṣvami samākhyātaṃ svayambhūcaityaṃ uttamaṃ

evaṃ vijñāya yo dhīmānvrataṃ caritumicchati|sa svayambhū jinakṣetraṃ āśritya caratāṃ vrataṃ

svayambhūkṣetramāśritya yaścarati vrataṃ mudā|sa labhet tanmahat puṇyamakṣayaṃ bodhisādhanaṃ

etat puṇyaṃ viśuddhātmā bhadraśrī sadguṇāśrayaḥ|bodhisattvo mahābhijñā bhavajjinātmajo dhruvaṃ

durgatiṃ na brajet kvāpi saṃsāre sa kadācana|sadā sadgatisaṃjāto bodhicaryāvrataṃ caret

evaṃ sa saṃsaralloke kṛtvā sarvatra bhadratāsaṃbodhi praṇidhiṃ dhṛtvā saṃcare tadjagaddhite

evaṃ ca bodhi saṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|trividhāṃ bodhimāsādyaḥ nirvṛtipadamāpnuyāt

evaṃ yūyaṃ parijñātvāḥ svayambhūsthāna āśritā|triratnaśaraṇaṃ gatvā saṃcaratha vratottamaṃ

etattenārhatādiṣṭaṃ śrutvā sa sugatātmajaḥ|jayaśriyaṃ tamarhantaṃ sampaśyannevamabravīt

bhadanta bhavatā diṣṭaṃ śrutvā me rocate manaḥ|svayambhūcaityamārādhya carituṃ vratamābhavaṃ

svayambhū caityarājaḥ śrīdharmadhātu jinālayaḥ|kutrāstyatra mahīloke tat samādeṣṭumarhati

iti saprārthitaṃ tena śrutvāṃ so'rhan yatiḥ sudhīḥ|jayaśrī staṃ mahāsattva samālokyaivamādiśata

vidyate'tra mahīloke uttarasyāṃ himālaye|nepāla iti vikhyāteḥ gopucchākhyernagottamaḥ

tadgirernāma cāturdhya caturyugeṣu vartate|tadyathābhūdyuge satye padmagiririti smṛtaḥ

tretāyāṃ vajrakūṭākhyo gośṛṃgo dvāpare smṛtaḥ|idānīṃ tu kalau'sau gopuccha iti viśrutaḥ

so'piśailo idānītu lokai nepāla deśakaiḥ|sāmheguriti vikhyāta stathāsaṃprasthito bhuvi

sa sarvadhāturalādi sarvadravyamayoktamaḥ|aśvattha pramukhairsavairpādapaiḥ saṃpraśobhitaḥ

sarvatra kusumairkāntaḥ saṃrvoṣadhiphaladrumaiḥ|sarvapakṣivirāvaiścaḥ bhramadbhramaranisvanaiḥ

jantubhiḥ sakalaḥ sneha nivaddhamaitramānasaiḥ|tapasvivaddhayā bhadracāribhiḥ saniṣevitaḥ

aṣṭāṃga guṇasaṃpanna saṃśuddhāmṛtanirjharaiḥ|śobhitaḥ puṣpagaṃdhādhi saṃvāsitaiḥ samīraṇaiḥ

saṃsevitaḥ sadā divya mahotsāhairvivājitaḥ|sarvalokādhipairnityaṃ saṃsevitaḥ samādaraiḥ

tatra ratnamaya padmaḥ karṇṇikāyāṃ samāśritaḥ|divya sphaṭikaratnābhajyotirūpo nirañjanaḥ

ekahastaḥ pramānoccaścaityarūpo jināśrayaḥ|svayambhūḥ sarvalokānāṃ bhadrārtha samavasthitaḥ

brahma śakrādibhirdevaiḥ sarvaillokādhipairapi|sarvairdaityādhipaiścāpi nāgendraiḥ garuḍairapi

siddhai vidyādharaiḥ sādhyairūryakṣagandharvakinnaraiḥ|rākṣasendraiśca rudraiśca grahaistārāgaṇairapi

vasubhiścāpsarobhiścaḥ sarvaiśva tridaśādhipaiḥ|ṛṣibhiryatibhiḥ sarvai yogibhi brahmacāribhiḥ

sarvaiśca tīrthikai vijñaistāpasaiścāpisajjanaiḥ|divāniśaṃ catuḥ saṃdhyaṃ dṛṣṭvā smṛtvā pravanditaḥ

nityakālaṃ samāgate samabhyarcya samādarāt|saṃstutibhiḥ mahotsāhaiḥ saṃmānitābhivanditaḥ

evaṃ sa trijagannāthaḥ svayambhū dharmadhātukaḥ|sarvaloka hitārthena saṃbhāṣayansamāsthitaḥ

idānīntu kalau lokā duṣṭāḥ krūrāśayā śaṭhāḥ|dṛṣṭavedaṃ dharmadhātuṃ hi hariṣyanti na saṃśayaḥ

ityasau śilayāchādya gupti kṛtvā prakāśitaḥ|tadupariṣṭīkābhiśca vidhāya caityamuttama

chātradhvajapatākābhiralaṃkṛtyādhyatiṣṭhataṃ|tatrāpi sarvalokaiśca sarvalokādhipairapi

samāgatya samārādhya samabhyarcyābhivanditaḥ|satkāraiśca mahotsāhaiḥ stutipradakṣiṇādibhiḥ

praṇāmaiśca samārādhya sevito mānito'rcitaḥ|evaṃ sa trijagannātho dharmadhātu jinālayaḥ

sarvasattva śubhārthena saṃśobhitā vyavasthitaḥ|tatra ye śaraṇaṃ gatvā yānti śuddhayā mudā

durggatiṃ te na gachanti saṃsāre'tra kadācanasadgatāveva saṃjātā dharmmaśrī saṃghaśobhitā

bodhisattvā mahāsattvāḥ pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|bhadraśrī sadguṇadhārā sarvasattva hitaṃkarāḥ

bodhicaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcare jagaddhite|evaṃ yūyamapi jñātvā śraddhayā śaraṇa gatāḥ

svayambhūcaityamārādhya saṃcaradhvaṃ vrataṃ sadā|evaṃ kṛtvātra saṃsāre bhadraśrī sadguṇāśrayā

bodhisattvā mahāsattvā jinātmajā bhaviṣyathaḥ|tataḥ saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpsyathaḥ|iti tena samādiṣṭaṃ niśaṃmya sa mahāmatiḥ

śāstāraṃ taṃ samālokya punarevamavocata|bhadanta śrotumicchāmi yatsvayambhū jinālayaḥ

kadā svayaṃ samutpannaḥ kathaṃ ca tadupādiśa|iti saṃprārthita tena śrutvā so'rhan yatiḥ sudhīḥ

jayaśrīstaṃ mahāsattvaṃ saṃpaśyannevamādiśat|sādhu śrṛṇu mahābhāga yathā mayā śrutaṃ guroḥ

tathāhaṃ te pravakṣāmi svayambhūtpattisatkathāṃ|tadyathā pāṭalīputre pure'śoko narādhipaḥ

saddharma sādhanotsāhī triratnasevako'bhavat|sa tatra kukkuṭārāme vihāre sugatāśrame

upaguptaṃ mahābhijñaṃ vandituṃ samupācaret|tadā so'rhan mahābhijñaḥ sarvasaṃghapuraskṛtaḥ

sabhāmadhyāsanāsīnastasthau dhyātvā samāhitaḥ|tamarhataṃ sabhāsīnaṃ sarvasaṃghapuraskṛtaḥ

dṛṣṭvāśokaḥ sa bhūmīndro muditaḥ samupācarat|tatra sa sahasopetya sāñjalirarhato yatīn

sarvānnatvopaguptaṃ tamaṣṭāṃgaiḥ prāṇamanmudā|tatastaṃ gurumarhantaṃ mahotsāhaiḥ yathāvidhiṃ

samabhyarcya praṇatvā ca dharmaṃ śrotumupāśrayat|tathā tatmantriṇaḥ sarve sāmātyasacivo janāḥ

tamarhantaṃ yatinnatvā tatraikāntamupāśrayat|tataḥ so'rhan mahābhijño dṛṣṭvā sarvā samāśritān

ādimadhyāntakalyāṇaṃ saddharmasamupādiśat|tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pītvā sarve lokāḥ pravodhitāḥ

dharmaviśeṣamājñāya saṃbodhivratamīcchire|tataḥ so'pi mahārājaḥ śrutvā taddharmamuttamaṃ

saṃbodhisādhanocaryā saṃcarituṃ samaicchata|tataḥ sa nṛpatī rājā sāñjaliḥ samupāśritaḥ

tamarhantaṃ mahāsattvaṃ natvā paśyaṃ mudāvadat|bhadanta śrotumicchāmi saṃbodhisādhanaṃ vrataṃ

kutra puṇyatamaṃ kṣatraṃ yatrāśu sidhyate vrataṃ|tad bhavān samupādiśya sarvāṃllokān prabodhayan

bodhimārge samāyujya saṃcārayitumarhati||iti saṃprārthite rājñā śrutvā so'rhan mahāmatiḥ

tamaśokaṃ mahīpālaṃ sampaśyannevamabravīt|sādhu śṛṇu mahārāja yathā me guruṇoditaṃ

tathāhaṃ te pravakṣāmi bodhivrataṃ yadīcchasi|sarvakṣatrottamo rājannuttarasyāṃ himālaye

nepāle iti vikhyāto yatrāśu sidhyate vrataṃ|tatrāpyati mahatpuṇyakṣatraṃ buddhaiḥ praśaṃsitaṃ

svayambhūcaityarājasya dharmadhātoḥ samāśrayaṃ|tatra yadyatkṛtaṃ karma tattatsaṃsidhyate dutaṃ

iti sarvai mahāsattvaiḥ saṃsevitaṃ jinairapi|iti vijñāya rājendra sambodhiṃ yadi vāñchasi

taccaityaśaraṇaṃ gatvā saṃcarasva susaṃvaraṃ|etatpuṇyaviśuddhātmā bhadraśrī sadguṇarddhimān

bodhisattvo mahābhijño bhaveḥ sarvahitārthabhṛt|tataḥ kramena sambodhiṃ saṃbhāraṃ paripūrayan

niḥkleśo'rhañjagannāthāḥ sambuddhapadamāpsyasi|iti tenārhatādiṣṭaṃ niśamya sa nṛpo mudā

upaguptaṃ guruṃ natvā papracchaivaṃ samādarāt|bhadanta śrotumichāmi svayambhūtpatti satkathāṃ

kadā svayaṃ samutpannaṃ statsamādeṣṭumarhati|iti saṃprārthitaṃ rājñā śrutvā so'rhan yatiḥ surdhīḥ

aśokaṃ taṃ mahīpālaṃ sampaśyannevamādiśat|sādhu rājan yathādiṣṭaṃ guruṇā me śrutaṃ mayā

tathāhaṃ saṃpravakṣāmi śṛṇuṣva tatsamāhitaḥ|tadā cāsau jagacchāstā śākyamunistathāgataḥ

sarvajño dharmarājo'rhan munīśvaro vināyakaḥ|sa sarvaḥ sāṃdhikaiḥ sārddhaṃ janapadeṣu saṃcaran

ekasmin samaye tatra nepāle samupācarat|gopucchaparvvatapārśve paścime śrīsvayambhūvaḥ

pucchāgro'bhidha caityasyasaṃnnidhau sugatāśrame|sarvasattvahitārthena pūrṇanduriva bhāsayan

saddharmma samupārdeṣṭuṃ vijahāra sasāṃdhikaḥ|yadā sa bhagavāñchāstān sattvānāṃ dharmma vṛddhaye

saddharmma samupādeṣṭuṃ sabhāsane samāśrayat|taṃ dṛṣṭvā tadā tatra mañjuśriyaḥ mamāśrame

vihāre vāsinī cūḍābhidhānī brahmacāriṇī|arhantī bhikṣuṇī bhadrā saddharmmaguṇavāñchinī

suprasannāśrayā śuddhā kāṣāya cīvarāvṛtā|divyapūjopacārāṇi samādāya pramoditā

tatsaddharmāmṛtaṃ pātuṃ tatrāśu samupācarat|tadā tatra mahāsattvā bodhisattvā jinātmajāḥ

maitreyapramukhāḥ sarve taddharma śrotumāgatāḥ|arhanto bhikṣavaścāpi śrāvakā brahmacāriṇaḥ

bhikṣuṇyo brahmacāriṇyaḥ cailakā vratino'pi ca|triratnaśaraṇāsīnāścopāsakā upāsikāḥ

bauddhabhaktiratāḥ sarve taddharmaśrotumāgatāḥ|sarve te samupāyātāstatra sabhāsanāśritaṃ

saṃbuddhaṃ taṃ samālokya muditāḥ samupācaran|te sarve'bhyarcya taṃ nāthaṃ natvā sāñjalayormudā

tatsaddharmāmṛta pātuṃ tatsabhāyāṃ samāśrayan|brahmaśakrādayā devāḥ sarve lokādhipā api

grahāstārāgaṇāścāpi siddhā vidyādharā api|sādhyā rudrāśca gandharvā yakṣaguhyakakinnarāḥ

daityendrā rākṣasendrāca nāgendrā garuḍā api|evamanyepi lokendrāḥ sarve tatra mudāgatāḥ

taṃ munīndraṃ samabhyarcya natvā tatra samāśrayan|hāritī yakṣiṇī cāpi bodhisattvānupālinī

sā bhavantaṃ tamānasya tatsabhāyāṃ samāśrita|atha sa bhagavānañchāstā dṛṣṭvā sarvān samāśritān

maitreyaṃ samupāmantrya sampaśyannevamādiśat|maitreyemaṃ jagannāthaṃ svayambhūvaṃ jinālayaṃ

dharmadhātuṃ triratnāyaṃ paśyadhvaṃ yūyamādarāt|bhajadhvaṃ śraddhayā nityaṃ gatvātra śaraṇaṃ
mudā

bodhicaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcarante jagaddhite|śraddhayā ye bhajantyatra śaraṇaṃ samupāśritāḥ

bodhicaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcarante jagaddhite|sarve vimuktapāpāste pariśuddha trimaṇḍalāḥ

bodhisattvā mahābhijñāḥ bhaveyuḥ sadguṇāśrayāḥ|bhadraśrīsukhasampannaścaturbrahmavihārikāḥ

sarvasattva hitodyuktāḥ saṃbodhivratacāriṇaḥ|na kvāpi durgatiṃyāyuḥ sadā sadgatisaṃbhavāḥ

triratnabhajanotsāhāḥ saddharmmasādhanodyamāḥ|tataḥ pravrajyā saṃbuddhaśāsane śaraṇaṃ gatāḥ

brahmacaryāṃ vrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃcareran samāhitāḥ|tataste nirmalātmāno niḥkleśāḥ vijitendriyāḥ

trividhāṃ bodhimāsādha sambuddhapadmāpnuyuḥ|evaṃ vijñāya ye martyā vāñchanti saugataṃ padaṃ

te bhajantu sadātraiva śraddhayā śaraṇāśritāḥ|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa niśamya te sabhāśritāḥ

sarve lokāḥ pramodantastathā bhajitumīcchīre|maitreyaḥ sa tato dhīmān bodhisattva hi moditaḥ

samutthāya munīndrasya purataḥ samupācaran|udvahannuktarāsaṃgaṃ praṇatvā taṃ munīśvaraṃ

jānubhyāṃ bhuvi saṃghāya sāñjaliretamavravīta|bhagavān nāthaḥ sarvajña dharmadhātujinālayaḥ

kadāyaṃ svayaṃmutpanna stansamādeṣṭumarhati|iti saṃprārthite tena maitreyeṇa mahātmanā

bhagavantaṃ mahāvijñaṃ saṃpaśyannevamādiśata|sādhu śṛṇu samādhāya maitreyo'sya svayambhūvaḥ

samutpattikathāṃ vakṣe sarvalokābhibodhane|purāsmin bhadrakalpe'bhūd vipaśvī nāma sarvavit

jagachāstā munīndro'rhan dharmmarājastathāgataḥ|aśīti varṣa sāhasra paramāyūṃṣi yadā nṛṇāṃ

tadāhaṃ satya dharmmākhyā bodhisattvā'bhavaṃt kila|yadā sa bhagavāñchāstā vandhumatyāḥ puro'ntike

vihāre dharmmamādiśca vijahāra sasāṃdhikaḥ|tadāhaṃ taṃ jagannāthamārādhya samupasthitaḥ

tadātrā bhūtsaptakośa vyāyāmavistaro hradaḥ|tadanuśāsanāṃ dhṛtvā prācaraṃ bodhi samvaraṃ

aṣṭāṃga guṇa sampannaḥ jalāśrayo nagāvṛtaḥ|padmotpalādi saugandhi nānā puṣpa praśobhitaḥ

haṃsa sārasa kāḍamba pramukha pakṣimaṇḍitaḥ|tīropāntanagāruha sarvarttu puṣpitairdrumaiḥ

phalauṣadhādi bṛkṣaiśca samantāt parimaṇḍitaḥ|mīnakacchapamaṇḍūkapramukha jalavāsināṃ

jantūnāṃ nilayo'gādhaḥ sarvanāgādhipālayaḥ|tatra sarvāhirājendraḥ karkkoṭakābhidho mahān

evaṃ tadā mahā tīrthaḥ puṇyāmṛtāśrayo vabhau|sadā tratridaśāḥ saddharmapsarobhiḥ pramoditāḥ

snātvā saṃkrīḍamānāḥ satsaukhyaṃ bhuktvā divaṃ yayuḥ|tathā brahmādayaḥ sarve maharṣayastapaśvinaḥ

snāna saṃdhyādikaṃ karmma kṛtvā saṃsevire sadā|evaṃ lokādhipāścāpi snātvātra sarvadā mudā

svasva kule samabhyarcya mahotsāhairnisevire|evaṃmanyepi lokāśca vratino brahmacāriṇaḥ

snātvātra saṃvaraṃ dhṛtvāḥ pūtātmāno divaṃ yayuḥ|bodhisattvā tathānaike snānadānavrataṃ mudā

kṛtvātra vimalātmānaḥ samācārañjagaddhite|evaṃ sarve munīndraiśca snāna vratādi bījaṃphalaṃ

mahatpuṇyataraṃ śreṣṭhamākhyātaṃ bodhisādhanaṃ|yatra snātvā triratnānāṃ śaraṇe samupāśritāḥ

bodhicaryā vrataṃ dhṛtvā prācaranta jagaddhite|te āśu vimalātmāno bhadraśrīsatguṇānvitāḥ

bodhisattvāḥ mahāsattvā vabhūvuḥ sugatātmajāḥ|kecinniḥ kleśitātmāno bhavasaṃcāre nispṛhāḥ

śrāvakabodhisattvamāsādya babhūvu brahmacāriṇaḥ|kecicca nirmalātmāno saṃsāre viratāśrayāḥ

pratyekabodhimāsādya sunirvṛtiṃ samāyayuḥ|kecit saṃbodhicittaṃ ca prāpya saddharmalālasāḥ

bodhicaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā samācarañjagaddhite|kecit sarve bhuktvā divyakāmasukhānyapi

saddharmaguṇasaṃraktāḥ prācaran sarvadā śubhe|kecit sarve mahīpālāḥ sunīti dharmacāriṇaḥ

kṛtvā sattvahitārthāni yayurante jinālayaṃ|ahamapi tadā tatra snātvācaraṃ vrataṃ sadā

etatpuṇyaviśuddhātmā drutaṃ sambodhimāptavān|yaiścāpyasya jalaṃ pītaṃ te'pi nirmuktapātakāḥ

pariśuddhāśayā bhadrā babhūvu bodhibhāginaḥ|evamasau mahātīrthaḥ sarvairapi munīśvaraiḥ

samadhiṣṭhāpito'dyāpi praśaṃsito mahītale|tatra paścāt svayaṃ dharmadhāturutpatsyate dhruvaṃ

ityādiśya munīndrau'sau bhūyā erevaṃ samādiśat|tadā tatra samupanne dharmadhātau jinālaye

nirutpātaṃ śubhotsāhaṃ pravartiṣyati sarvadā|sarve lokāśca taṃ dṛṣṭvā dharmadhātuṃ svayambhūvaṃ

śraddhayā śaraṇaṃ gatvā prabhajiṣyanti sarvadā|tadetatpuṇyaliptāste sarve lokāḥ śubhendriyāḥ

bhadraśrī satsukhaṃ bhuktvā yāsyantyante jinālayaṃ|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa vipaśvinā niśamyate

sarve sabhāśritā lokāḥ prābhyanandan pravodhitāḥ|iti vipaśvinā śāstā samādiṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ mayā

tathā yuṣmatprodhārtha samākhyātaṃ pravuddhyatāṃ|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa śrīghanena niśamyate

maitreyādi samālokāḥ sarve'pi saṃprasedire|iti svayambhūdharmadhātu samutpatti nidānakathā prathamo'dhyāyaḥ
samāptaḥ|
