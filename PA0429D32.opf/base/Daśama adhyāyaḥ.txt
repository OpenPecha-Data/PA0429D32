daśama adhyāyaḥśrī dharmmadhātusvayambhūtpattidharmmamāhātmyasubhāṣitasūtraṃ nāmaathāsau bhagavān
bhūyo maitreyaṃ taṃ mahāmatiṃ|samālokya sabhāṃ cāpi samāmantryaivamādiśat

śṛṇu maitreya vakṣyāmi mañjuśriyo jagadguroḥ|saddharmaguṇamāhātmyaṃ saṃbodhijñānadāyakaṃ

yadiyaṃ bhikṣuṇī cūḍā suśīlā brahmacāriṇī|idaṃ mañjuśriyaṃ ścaityaṃ śraddhayā samupāśritā

śuddhotpalasrajo nityaṃ samabhyarcya yathāvidhi|smṛtvā dhyātvā samārādhya sabhaktyā śraddhayā
sadā

ādyāṃ cale cule vande svāheti navamākṣaraṃ|dhāraṇī paramāvidyāṃ paṭhantī bhajane sadā

etanpuṇyānubhāvena cūṃḍeyaṃ bhikṣuṇī satī|pañcābhijñāvatī varṣairdvādaśabhirbhaved dhruvaṃ

tataśceyaṃ mahābhijñā śrīsamṛddhiguṇāśrayā|sarvasattvahitaṃ kṛtvā pracared bodhisaṃvaraṃ

tato'rhantī mahāprājñā pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalā|trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpsyati

evamanyepi lokāśca caityamañjuśriyo traye|paṭhantī dhāraṇīmenāṃ bhajanti śraddhayā sadā

te'pi sarve vikalmāṣāḥ pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|bhadraśrīsadguṇādhārā bodhisattvā jitendriyāḥ

pañcābhijñapadaprāptāścaturbrahmavihāriṇaḥ|sarvasattvahitādhārā careyurbodhisambaraṃ

tataste bodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|jitvā māragaṇān sarvān niḥkleśā vimalendriyāḥ

arhanto'pi mahābhijñāḥ saṃbodhisādhanāratāḥ|trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpnuyuḥ

yūyamapiti matvātra caityamañjuśriyastathā|paṭhanto dhāraṇīmenāṃ bhajadhvaṃ bodhimānasāḥ

etatpuṇyābhiliptā hi pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|yūyamapi tathā sarve bhaveta sugatātmajāḥ

bodhisattvā bhadraśrīsadguṇāśrayāḥ|mahābhijñā jagannāthā bhaveta bhadracāriṇaḥ

tataḥ saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|jitvā māragaṇān sarvāścaturbrahmavihāriṇaḥ

arhantastrividhāṃ bodhiṃ prāpya buddhā bhaviṣyatha|iti satyaṃ parijñāya yadi saṃbodhimicchatha

asmin mañjuśriyaścaityaṃ bhajadhvaṃ sarvadā mudā|ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa niśamya te sasāṃdhikāḥ

sarvalokāstathetyuktvā prābhyanandat prabodhitāḥ|tataḥ sarve'pi te lokā brahmāśakrādayo'marāḥ

sarve lokādhipāścāpi sārddhaṃ parijanairmudā|bhagavantaṃ munīndraṃ taṃ sasaṃgha saṃprasāditāḥ

natvā pradakṣiṇī kṛtvā svasvālayaṃ mudā yayuḥ|sarve martyā nṛpādyāśca samantrijanapaurikāḥ

sasāṃdhikaṃ munīndraṃ taṃ natvā svasvālayaṃ yayuḥ|hārīti yakṣiṇīśāpi sātmajā bauddharakṣaṇī

triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā dharmadhātorūpāśrayat|tataḥ sa bhagavāṃścāpi samutthāya sasāṃdhikaḥ

prabhāsayañjagadbhāsā jaṭodyānāśrame yayau|tatra sa trijagannātho vihāre sahasāṃdhikaiḥ

saddharmmasamupādiśya vijahāra jagaddhite|iti te guruṇādiṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ mayā tathocyate

śrutvāpyetanmahārājā śraddhayābhyanumodaya|iti śāstrārhatādiṣṭaṃ niśamya sa narādhipaḥ

prasāditastamarhanta natvā prāhaivamādarāt|bhadantohaṃ samicchāmi saṃdraṣṭuṃ taṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ

tannaipāle pragacchāmi tadanujñāṃ pradehi me|iti saṃprārthitaṃ rājñā śrutvā sorhanyatirmudā

nṛpatiṃ taṃ mahāsattvaṃ saṃpaśyannevamādiśyat|sādhu rājan samicchā te yadyasti taṃ svayambhuvaṃ

draṣṭuṃ gaccha samārādhya bhaja śraddhāsamanvitaḥ|sarvatīrtheṣu ca snātvā datvā dānaṃ yathepsitaṃ

vītarāgāṃ samārādhya samabhyarcyabhijādarāt|dharmodayāṃ mahādevīṃ khagānanāṃ jineśvarīṃ

śraddhayā samupāśritya samabhyarcya bhajādarāt|pañcapurāsthitāḥ pañca devatāśca yathāvidhi

samārādhya samabhyarcya bhaja bhaktyā samādarāt|mañjudevasya caityaṃ ca samālokya yathāvidhi

samārādhya samabhyarcya bhajenāṃ dhāraṇīṃ paṭhan|ācārya ca guhāsīnaṃ samādhidhyānasaṃsthitaṃ

dhyātvārādhya samabhyarcya natvā bhaja samādarāt|evamanyān mahāsattvān dharmmadhātorūpāsakān

sarvānapi samārādhya samabhyarcya praṇāmaya|etatpuṇyaviśuddhātmā bhadraśrī sadguṇāśrayaḥ

bodhisattvā mahāsattvā jagadbharttā bhavedapi|tataḥ saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

arhansaṃbodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpsyasi|iti satyaṃ parijñāya saṃbodhi yadi vāñchasi

gatvā tatra mahotsāhairdharmmadhātuṃ vilokya taṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya bhajasva samupāśritaḥ

gaccha te maṅgalaṃ bhuyāt sidhyatu te samīhitaṃ|yathecchayā samālokya samāyāhi pramoditaḥ

iti śāstrā samādiṣṭaṃ śrutvā sa nṛpatirmudā|taṃ guruṃ sāñjalirnatvā prāpyānujñāmanandataḥ

tataḥ sa nṛpatī rājā samantrijanapaurikāḥ|rājarddhi maṃgalotsāhaiḥ saṃprasthito mudācarat

tatra mārge sa rājendraḥ sarvān lokān prasādayan|mahotsāhaiścarannāśu naipālaṃ samupāyayau

tatra prāptaḥ samālokya dūrāt taṃ śrīsvayaṃmbhuvaṃ|sāñjali praṇatiṃ kṛtvā pramanāḥ sahasā caret

tatra sarvatra saṃvīkṣya śubhotsāhapravarttitaṃ|vismayānanditānsā sa nṛpatiḥ samupāsaret

tatra sarveṣu tīrtheṣu krameṇa sa narādhipaḥ|snātvārthibhyo yathākāmaṃ dadau dānaṃ caran vrataṃ

tato'ṣṭau vītarāgān sa nṛpatirvīkṣya harṣitaḥ|yathāvidhi samārādhya bhajatyarcya yathāvidhi

tato mudācaran vīkṣya dharmmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajan kramāt

tato vāyupure vāyudevatāṃ sagaṇāṃ mudā|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyacyanitobhajata

tataścāgnipure vahnidevatāḥ sagaṇāmapi|yathāvidhi samārādhya saṃpūjyābhajadādarāt

tato nāgapure nāgadevatāḥsagaṇā api|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyā mudābhajat

tato vasupure devīṃ sagaṇāṃ śrīvasundharāṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya bhajatyarcya samādarāt

tataḥ śāntipure śrīmatsambaraṃ sagaṇaṃ tathā|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcya mudābhajat

tataḥ śāntikarācārya samādhidhyānasaṃsthitaṃ|dhyātvārādhya samabhyarcya prābhajan saṃpramoditaḥ

tato dharmmodayā devī khagānanāṃ maheśvarīṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcya mudābhajat

sambuddhaṃ puṇḍarīkākṣaṃ sarvajña karuṇāspadaṃ|samantabhadraśāstāraṃ śākyasiṃhaṃ namāmyahaṃ

śrīghanaṃ śrīmatiṃ śreṣṭhaṃ śīlarāśiṃ śivakaraṃ|śrīmantaṃ śrīkaraṃ śāntaṃ śāntimūrti namāmyaham

nairātmavādinaṃ siṃhaṃ niravadyaṃ nirāśravaṃ|nītijñaṃ nirmalātmānaṃ niṣkalaṃkaṃ namāmyaham

nirdvandvaṃ nirahaṃkāraṃ nirvikalpaṃ tathāgataṃ|nirddhūtanikhilakleśaṃ niṣprapaṃcaṃ namāmyahaṃ

viśveśvaraṃ viśeṣo'haṃ viśvarūpaṃ vināyakaṃ|viśvalakṣaṇasaṃpūrṇṇa vītarāgaṃ namāmyahaṃ

vidhāvareṇa saṃpannaṃ viśveśamvimalaprabhaṃ|vinītavegaṃ vimalaṃ vītamohaṃ namāmyahaṃ

dudantidamakaṃ śāntaṃ śuddhaṃ pañcajinālayaṃ|sugatiṃ suśrutaṃ saumyaṃ śubhrakīrtti namāmyahaṃ

yogīśvaraṃ daśabalaṃ lokajñaṃ lokapūjitaṃ|lokācārya lokamūrtiṃ lokānāthaṃ namāmyahaṃ

kalaṃkamuktiṃ kāmāriṃ sakalaikaṃ kalādharaṃ|kāntamūrti dayāpātraṃ kanakābhaṃ namāmyahaṃ

tato mañjuśriyaścaityaṃ dharmadhātumupāśrayan|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyānatobhajat

tato mudā caran vīkṣya dharmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajan mudā

bhaktyā paramayāstauṣījinālayaṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ|jyotīrūpāya caitanyaṃ rūpāya bhavate namaḥ

murādinidhanāya śrīdātre praṇavarupiṇe|viśvatomukharupāya bhaktavatsala te namaḥ

pṛthvyādibhūtanirmātre jagadvaṃdyāyate namaḥ|jagatsraṣṭe jagatpātre jagaddhartre namo namaḥ

dhyānagamyāya dhyeyāya cartuvargapradāyine|evaṃ stutvā aśokaḥ sa punaḥ kṣamāpanaṃ vyadhāt

evaṃ sa nṛpatiḥ sarvān dharmadhātorupāsakān|mahāsattvān samabhyarcya satkṛtya samatoṣayat

evaṃ sa nṛparājaḥ śrī dharmadhatorūpāśritaḥ|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā prācarad bodhisambaraṃ

tataḥ sa nandito rājā samantrijanapaurikaḥ|natvā pradakṣiṇīkṛtya dharmmadhātuṃ mudācarat

tataścaran sa bhūmīndro mahotsāhaiḥ pramoditaḥ|sahasā puramāsādya vihāra samupācarat

tatropetya tamarhantamupaguptasasāṃdhikaṃ|samīkṣya sañjalirnatvā sabhaikāntaṃ samāśrayat

taṃ samāyātamālokya so'rhe śāstā prasannadṛk|svāgataṃ kuśalaṃ kaccinnṛpatiṃ paryapṛcchata

tacchutvā sa mahīpālaḥ śāstāraṃ taṃ kṛtāñjaliḥ|praṇatvā suprasannāsyaḥ saṃpaśyannevamabravīt

samāgatosmyahaṃ śāstarbhavatkṛpānubhāvataḥ|kuśalaṃ me kathaṃ na syāt sarvatrāpi sadāpi hi

bhavatkṛpānubhāvena nepāle'haṃ mudācaran|dṛṣṭvā sarveṣu tīrtheṣu snātvā dānaṃ yathepsitaṃ

tathāṣṭau vītarāgāśca samālokya pramāditaḥ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyabhijaṃ kramāt

tataḥ samīkṣya taṃ śrīmaddharmmadhātuṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajan
mudā

tato vāyupure vāyudevatābhyarccitā mayā|tataścāgnipure vahnirdevatāpi mayārccitā

tathā nāgapure nāgarājāṃścāpi mayārccitā|tathā tathā vasupure devī vasundharāṃ samarccitā

tataḥ śāntipure śrīmatsambaraśca samarccitaḥ|tataḥ śāntikarācāryaḥ samālokya mayārcitaḥ

devīṃ khagānanāṃ cāpi samārādhya samarcitā|mañjudevasya caityaṃ ca yathāvidhi samarcitaṃ

evaṃ bhadanta tatropacchandohe puṇyabhūtale|yathāvidhi samārādhya sarvadevā mayārcitāḥ

etatpuṇyaṃ mayā labdhaṃ bhavatkṛpānubhāvataḥ|tadatra janmasāphalyaṃ jīvitaṃ cāpi me'dhunā

tathātra sarvadā śāsta dharmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|smṛtvā nāma samuccārya dhyātvā bhajeya mā bhavaṃ

iti rājñā samākhyātaṃ śrutvā so'rha prasāditaḥ|nṛpati taṃ samālokya punarevaṃ samādiśat

dhanyo'si yatmahārājadharmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|smṛtvā dhyātvāpi saṃbhaktuṃmicchase'tra sadā bhaja

etatpuṇyaviśuddhātmā bhadraśrīsadguṇāśrayaḥ|bodhisattvo mahāsattvaḥ sarvadharmādhipo bhaveḥ

tataḥ saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|arhanstribodhimāsādya dhruvaṃ buddhapadaṃ labheḥ

samutpattikathāṃ tasya dharmadhātoḥ svayaṃbhuvaḥ|śrutvāpi yatmahatpuṇyaṃ saṃbodhisādhanaṃ labhet

iti matvā samutpattikathāṃ tasya svayaṃbhuvaḥ|satkṛtya śraddhayā martyāḥ śrotumarhanti sarvathā

samutpattikathāṃ tasya dharmadhātoḥ svayaṃbhuvaḥ|śṛṇvanti ye narā bhaktyā satkṛtya śraddhayā
mudā

durgatiṃ te na gacchanti kutrāpi hi kadācan|sadā sadgatisaṃjātā bhadraśrīsadguṇāśrayāḥ

sarvasattvahitādhānabodhicaryāvratāratāḥ|mahābhijñā jagannāthā bhaveyuḥ sugatātmajāḥ

krameṇa bodhisambhāraṃ pūrayitvā jagaddhite|arhantatrividhāṃ bodhiṃ prāpyeyuḥ saugataṃ padaṃ

iti matvā mahārāja śrotavyaṃ śraddhayādarāt|svayambhūguṇamāhātmyaṃ durllabhaṃ bodhivāñchibhiḥ

śṛṇvanti ye narā bhaktyā satkṛtya śraddhayā mudā|svayaṃbhūguṇamāhātmyasaddharmaśrīguṇārthadaṃ

te'pi na durgatiṃ yāyuḥ sadāsadgatisaṃgatāḥ|bhadraśrīsadguṇādhārā bhaveyurbodhilābhinaḥ

tasmādetatmahatpuṇyaṃ śrutvā dhyātvā sadādarāt|smṛtvā nāma samuccārya saṃbhaktavyaṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ

ahamapi purā rājan dharmmadhātoḥ svayaṃbhuvaḥ|śrutvā sadguṇasāṃkathyaṃ babhūva saṃpramoditaḥ

tato'haṃ sahasā tatra nepāle samupācaraṃt|snātvā sarveṣu tīrtheṣu dadau dānaṃ yathepsitaṃ

aṣṭau tān vītarāgāṃśca samārādhya mayārcitāḥ|devīṃ khagānanāṃ cāpi samārādhya samarcitā

mañjudevasya caityaṃ ca samārādhya samarcitaṃ|pañca purā sthitā pañca devatāśca samarcitāḥ

tathā śāntikarācāryaḥ samālokya samarcitaḥ|tataḥśaraṇamāśritya dharmadhāto svayaṃbhuvaḥ

satkṛtya śraddhayā nityaṃ samārādhya mudā bhajaṃ|evaṃ tatra sadāśritya dharmadhātorūpāsakaḥ

triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā prācaran bodhisaṃbaraṃ|etatpuṇyaviśuddhātmā pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalaḥ

ahaṃ bodhiṃ samāsādya jinātmajā bhave'dhunā|iti vijñāya mānuṣyā vāñchanti ye sunirvṛtiṃ

dharmadhātuṃ samārādhya bhajantu te sadā mudā|smṛtvā dhyātvā ca nāmāpi samuccārya samādarāt

samālokya praṇatvāpi bhajantu taṃ jinālayaṃ|ye bhajanti sadā smṛtvā dhyātvā natvā jinālayaṃ

drutaṃ saṃbodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpnuyuḥ|iti satyaṃ samākhyātaṃ sarvairapi munīśvaraiḥ

śrutvānumodya taṃ dharmadhātuṃ smṛtvā bhajantvalaṃ|subhāṣitamidaṃ yepi śrutvānumoditāśayāḥ

dharmadhātumanusmṛtvā dhyātvā bhajanti sarvadā|te'pi caitatmahatpuṇyapariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ

bhadraśrīsadguṇādhārāścaturbrahmavihāriṇaḥ|bodhisattvā mahāsattvā mahābhijñāḥ śubhendriyāḥ

drutaṃ saṃbodhimāsādya saṃbuddhapadamāpnuyuḥ|iti me guruṇādiṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ mayā tathocyate

tvamapīdaṃ sadā lokā śrāvayitvānumodaya|etatpuṇyānubhāvena sarvatra sarvadāpi te

nirutpātaṃ śubhotsāhaṃ bhavennūnaṃ narādhipaḥ|iti tenārhatādiṣṭaṃ śrutvāśoko nṛpo mudā

tatheti prativijñapya prābhyanandat sapārṣadaḥ|tataḥ sarve'pi te lokā niśamyaitatsubhāṣitaṃ

anumodya mahotsāhaiḥ saṃcerire śubhe sadā|tadaitatpuṇyabhāvena sarvatra tatra sarvadā

nirutpātaṃ śubhotsāhaṃ prāvarttata nirantaraṃ|ityādiśya mahābhijño jayaśrīḥ sa mahāmatiḥ

sarvānstān sāṃdhikān paśyan punarevaṃ samādiśat|yatredaṃ dharmmasāṃkathyaṃ prāvarttayet kalāvapi

bhāṣetya śṛṇuyādyaśca śrāvayedyaḥ pracārayet|eteṣāṃ tatra sarveṣāṃ saṃbuddhāḥ sakalāḥ sadā

kṛpā dṛṣṭyā samālokya prakuryurbhadramābhavaṃ|sarvā pāramitā devyasteṣāṃ tatra sadā śivaṃ

kṛtvā saṃbodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayeyuryathākramaṃ|sarve'pi bodhisattvāśca pratyekasugatā api

arhanto yoginasteṣāṃ prakuryurmaṅgalaṃ sadā|sarve lokādhipāścāpi sarve cāpi maharṣayaḥ

tatra teṣāṃ hi sarveṣāṃ kuryuḥ samīkṣya maṅgalaṃ|sarve devādhipāścāpi sarvadaityādhipā api

tathā sarve'pi gandharvāḥ sarvayakṣādhipā api|garuḍā nāgarājāśca kumbhāṇḍādhīśvarā api

samīkṣya sarvadā teṣāṃ rakṣāṃ kuryuḥ samantataḥ|sarvāśca mātṛkā devyaḥ sabhairavagaṇā api

kṛtvā rakṣāṃ sadā teṣāṃ kuryurbhadraṃ samantataḥ|sarve grahāśca tārāśca siddhā vidyādharā api

sādhyāścāpi sadālokya teṣāṃ kuryuḥ sumaṅgalaṃ|bhūtapretapiśācāśca duṣṭā māragaṇā api

vīkṣya teṣāṃ prasannāste rakṣāṃ kuryuḥ sadā mudā|svayambhūguṇamāhātmya sāṃkathyaṃ yo'likhet
mudā

tenāpi likhitaṃ sarva mahāyānasubhāṣitaṃ|lekhāpitaṃ ca yenedaṃ dharmadhātusubhāṣitaṃ

tenāpi sakalaṃ sūtraṃ lekhāpitaṃ bhaved dhruvaṃ|likhitaṃ cāpi yecedaṃ pratiṣṭhāpya yathāvidhi

śuddhasthāne gṛhe sthāpya pūjāṅgaiḥ sarvadārcitaṃ|tenārhanto jināḥ sarve pratyekasugatā api

sasaṃghā bodhisattvāśca bhavanti pūjitāḥ khalu|yaścāpīdaṃ svayaṃ dhṛtvā parebhyo'pi samādiśat

bhāvayet satataṃ smṛtvā dhyātvāpi praṇamenmudā|tasya sarvamunīndro hi pratyekasugatā api

arhanto bodhisattvāśca tuṣṭā dadyuḥ samīhitaṃ|yaścaitadupadeṣṭāraṃ sarvāśca śrāvakānapi

yathāvidhi sa samabhyarcya bhājanaiḥ samatoṣayet|tena sarve'pi saṃbuddhāḥ pratyekasugatā api

arhanto bhikṣavaḥ sarve yogino brahmacāriṇaḥ|bodhisattvāśca sarve'pi vratino yatayo'pi ca

arcitā bhojitāstuṣṭā bhaveyuranumoditāḥ|kimevaṃ vahunoktena sarve buddhā munīśvarāḥ

sarvāstārāśca devyo'pi sarvasaṃghā jinātmajāḥ|nityaṃ teṣāṃ kṛpā dṛṣṭayā samālokyānumoditāḥ

rakṣāṃ vidhāya sarvatra varaṃ dadyuḥ samīhitaṃ

sarve lokādhipāścāpi sarve devā surādhipāḥ|rakṣāṃ kṛtvā varaṃ dadyusteṣāṃ saddharmasādhane

rājāno'pi sadā teṣāṃ rakṣā kṛtvānumoditāḥ|yathābhivāñchitaṃ datvā pālayeyuḥ sadādarāt

mantriṇo'pi sadā teṣāṃ sāmātyasacivānugāḥ|sabhṛtyasainyabhaṭṭāśca bhaveyurhitakāriṇaḥ

sarve vaiśyāśca sarvārthabharttāraḥsyu suhṛnpriyāḥ|śreṣṭimahājanāḥ sarve bhaveyurhitakāriṇaḥ

dviṣopi dāsatāṃ yāyurdṛṣṭāśca syurhitāśayāḥ|evamanyepi lokāśca servva syurmaitramānasāḥ

paśavaḥ pakṣiṇaścāpi sarvakīṭāśca jantavaḥ|naiva teṣāṃ viruddhā syuḥ bhaveyurhitaśaṃsina

evaṃ sarvatra lokeṣu teṣāṃ saddharmasādhināṃ|nirutpātaṃ śubhotsāhaṃ saumāṅgalyaṃ sadā bhavet

evaṃ bhadrataraṃ puṇyaṃ svayabhūṃbhavanodbhavaṃ|natvā taṃ trijagannāthaṃ bhajadhvaṃ sarvadā
mudā

ye tasya śaraṇe sthitvā smṛtvā dhyātvā samāhitāḥ|nāmāpi ca samuccārya bhajanti śraddhayā sadā

teṣāṃ trinyapi ratnāni suprasannāni sarvadā|kṛpā dṛṣṭayā samālokya kṛtvā deyuḥ sadā śubhaṃ

iti śāstrāsamādiṣṭaṃ jayaśriyā niśamyate|jineśvarī pramukhāḥ saṃghā sarve nandanprabodhitāḥ

sarvāvatī sabhā sāpi śrutvaitat saṃprasāditāḥ|tatheti prativijñapya prābhyanandan prabodhitāḥ

tataste sakalā lokāḥ samutthāya prasāditāḥ|jayaśriya sasaṃghaṃ taṃ natvā svasvāśramaṃ yayuḥ

tatra nityamupetyatyarddhyā sasaṃghaḥ sajinātmajā|snātvā sarveṣu tīrtheṣu dhṛtvā vrataṃ yathāvidhi

tato'ṣṭau vītarāgāṃśca devīṃ cāpi khagānanāṃ|pañcamā devatāścāpi yathāvidhi samarccayan

tathā śāntikarācārya caitya mañjuśriyo'pi ca|dharmādhātuṃ samārādhya dhyātvābhyarcya sadābhajat

tadaitatpuṇyabhāvena viṣaye tatra sarvadā|nirutpātaṃ śubhotsāhaṃ prāvarttata samaṃtataḥ

tataścāsau mahābhijñā jayaśrīḥ sugatātmajaḥ|sarvānstān sāṃdhikān paśyan punarevaṃ samādiśat

yatredaṃ dharmasāṃkathyaṃ pracāritaṃ svayaṃbhuvaḥ|tatraittpuṇyabhāvena bhavatu sarvadā śubhaṃ

saṃbuddhāstatra sarve'pi pratyekasugatā api|arhanto bodhisattvāśca kurvantu maṃṅgalaṃ sadā

sarve lokādhipāścāpi sarve cāpi maharṣayaḥ|samālokya sadā tatra prakurvantu sumaṅgalaṃ

kāle varṣantu meghāśca bhūyācchaśyavatī mahī|nirutpātaṃ subhikṣyaṃ ca bhavantu tatra sarvadā

rājā bhavatu dhārmmiṣṭho mantriṇo nīticāriṇaḥ|sarve lokāḥsuvṛttiṣṭhā bhavantu dharmasādhinaḥ

sarve sattvāḥ samācārāḥ saṃbodhivihitāśayāḥ|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā saṃcarantāṃ sadā śubhe

iti jayaśriyādiṣṭaṃ śrutvā sarve'pi sāṃdhikāḥ|evamastviti prābhāṣya prābhyanandanprasāditāḥ

iti śrī dharmmadhātusvayambhūtpattidharmmamāhātmyasubhāṣitasūtraṃ daśama adhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ||
