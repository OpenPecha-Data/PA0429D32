tataśca te mahābhijñāḥ bhadraśrī satguṇānvitāḥ|valiṣṭhā duṣṭa jetāro bhaveyu stribhaveśvarāḥ

tataste trividhāṃ bodhimāsādya bhadracāriṇaḥ|sambodhijñānasadrantasamṛddhāḥ syu rmunīśvarāḥ

tataste sugatāḥ buddhāḥ daśabhūmīśvarājināḥ|bodhimārge pratiṣṭhāpya kūryuḥ sarvānsudharmmiṇaḥ

evaṃ dharmamayaṃ kṛtvā sarvatra bhuvaneṣvapi|sunivṛrtipadaṃ prāpya saṃprayāyurjinālayaṃ

evamasyā mahādevyā bhajano hūtamuktamaṃ|puṇyaṃ mahattaraṃ siddhaṃ sambuddhapadasādhanaṃ

iti satyaṃ samākhyātaṃ sarvai rapi munīśvaraiḥ|vijñāyāsyā mahādevyā bhajadhvaṃ śaraṇe sthitā

asyāpi dharma dhātauśca bhajanodbhūtamuktamaṃ|puṇyaṃ mahattaraṃ siddhaṃ sambuddhapadasādhanaṃ

asyāpi mañjudevasyaḥ bhajanod bhūtamuktamaṃpuṇyaṃ mahattaraṃ siddhaṃ sambuddhapadasādhanaṃ

matveti yadi vo vāṃcchā vidyate saugate pade|sarve yūyaṃ samādhāya bhajatāntra jinālaye

khagānanāsyāyā mahādevyāḥ śaraṇe ca samāśritāḥ|dhyātvā smṛtvā samuccārya nāmāpi bhajatābhavaṃ

ya eṣāṃ śaraṇe sthitvā dhyātvā smṛtvā samāhitāḥ|nāmāpi samudāhṛtya bhajanti sarvadābhave

te sabhadrā nagacchanti durggatiṃ ca kadācana|sadā sadgati saṃjātā bhaveyuḥ śrī guṇāśrayāḥ

bodhisattvā mahāsattvā saddharma bodhi cāriṇaḥ|sarvasattva hitodyuktā bhaveyuḥ sugatātmajāḥ

evanteṣāṃ mahatsaukhyaṃ bhadraśrī sadguṇānvitaṃ|sarvadāpi nirutpātaṃ pravarktate samāhitaḥ

daivotpātabhayanteṣāṃta vidyate samantataḥ|yaktāndṛṣṭvā surāḥ sarve rakṣantīndrādayomudā

agnyutpātaṃbhayaṃ teṣāṃ vidyate na kadācana|yat tāndṛṣṭvā prasīdanto rakṣeyurvahnayaḥ sadā

akāla maraṇād bhītisteṣānnavidyate kvacit|yadyamopi samalokya rakṣati tāṃ prasāditaḥ

rākṣasasyābhayaṃ teṣāṃ vidyate na samantataḥ|yad vāyavo'pi tā vījya rakṣeyuḥ sarvadānugāḥ

yakṣasyāpi bhayaṃ teṣāṃ vidyate na sadā kvacit|yakṣā samīkṣya rakṣeyuḥ sarve yakṣādhipā api

bhūtebhyo'pi bhayanteṣāṃ vidyate na samantataḥ|yadīśānopi tānpaśya saṃrakṣetsaṃpramoditāḥ

gandharvotpati to bhītisteṣānnavidyate kvacit|dhṛtarāṣṭo'pi tānpaśyat saṃrakṣetsaṃ prasāditaḥ

kumbhāṇḍebhyo'pi nāstyevaṃ bhayanteṣāṃ kadācana|viruḍhakohitān paśyanabhirakṣan prasādataḥ

nāgebhyo'pi bhayanteṣāṃ vidyate na sadā kvacita|virupākṣo hi samyaśyantānrakṣansamprasāditaḥ

yakṣebhyo'pi sadā teṣāṃ vidyate na bhayaṃ kvacita|kuvero hi samālokya saṃrakṣastān prasāditaḥ

bhayaṃ na kinnarebhyopi teṣāmasti kadācana|drumo hitān mahāsattvā saṃvīkṣavat prasāditaḥ

guhyakebhyopi nāstyeva bhayanteṣāṃ sudharmmaṇāṃ|vajrapāṇirhitānvīkṣya prasāditā bhavet sadā

tathā vidyādharebhyo'pi bhayanteṣānna vidyate|yataḥ sarvārthasiddho'pi samyaśyanstān vetsadā

grahotpāta bhayanteṣāṃ vidyate na kadācana|grahādhipā'hi sarve'pi samīkṣya tānavetsadā

tathā tārāgaṇotpātabhayaṃ teṣānnavidyate|sarvāstā rāhi tānvīkṣya sarvatrā veyurābhavaṃ

siddhāḥ sādhyāścarudrāśca vīkṣyāveyuḥ sadāpi tān|tattebhyopi bhayaṃ kvāpi teṣāṃ nāsti kadācana

tathā ca mātṛkotpātaṃ bhayaṃ nāsti kadācana|sarvāhi mātṛkān dṛṣṭvā rakṣeyu stāṃ prasāditāḥ

mahākālo gaṇeśa skandaśca bhairavā api|sarvadā tānsamālokya rakṣeyuḥ saṃprasāditāḥ

pretā bhūtāḥ piśāścāśca vetāḍā ḍākinīgaṇāḥ|api tānsarvadālokya saṃrakṣeyuḥ prasāditāḥ

tebhyo'pi bhayanteṣāṃ vidyate na kadācana|sarve'pi rtesahāyāḥ syu saṃvodhi dharmmasādhane

siṃhādi sarvajantubhyoḥ pakṣibhyo'pi samantataḥ|sarpādi kṛmikīṭebhyo bhayanteṣāṃ sadāpi na

duṣṭa pratyarthikebhyo'pi taskarebhyopi sarvataḥ|śatrubhyo'pi bhayanteṣāṃ vidyate na samantataḥ

yadetatpuṇyaliptāstāṃ samīkṣya te prasāditāḥ|sarve maitrī kṛpā sneha nivaddhāḥ syuhitārthinaḥ

evaṃ sarve'pi satvāstāndṛṣṭvā saṃprasāditāḥ|snigdhaciktāprasannāsyāḥ paśyeyu maitrībhāvataḥ

rājāno'pi ca tāndṛṣṭvā maitrī snehasabhāvitāḥ|suprasannāśayāḥ prītā mānayeyuḥ sadā mudā

mantrīṇo'pi sadā teṣāṃ maitrīsneha sabhāṣitāḥ|mānayeyuḥyathākāmaṃ saddharmma vratasādhanaṃ

brāmhaṇā api sarve ca teṣāṃ saddharmma sādhanaṃ|dṛṣṭvānumoditātmāno dadhu bhadrāśivaṃ sadā

ṛṣayo'pi tathā sarve tāṃ dṛṣṭvā saṃprasāditāḥ|paśyantaḥ kṛpayā dṛṣṭvā modayeyuḥ subhāśiṣā

evaṃ ca yoginaḥ siddhā yatayo bramhacāriṇaḥ|tīrthikāstāpasāścāpi vratinaścāpyupāsakāḥ

cairakā bhikṣavoścāpi bhikṣuṇyaścāpyupāsikāḥ|api tāṃ sudṛśālokya nandayeyuḥ śubhāśiṣāḥ

evaṃ ca śrāvakāḥ sarve pratyeka sugatā api|bodhisatvāśca sarve'pi tān dṛṣṭvā saṃprasāditāḥ

kṛpayā dṛṣṭavānupaśyanto maitrī snehasubhāvinaḥ|rakṣitvā bodhimārgeṣu niyājeyuḥ sadā bhavaṃ

evaṃ sarve'pi saṃbuddhā dṛṣṭvā tāṃ saṃprasāditāḥ|sarvadā kṛpayā rakṣyucārayeyurjjagaddhite

evaṃ teṣāṃ mahatpuṇyaṃ sambuddha padasādhanaṃ|bhadraśrīguṇasaṃpatti samṛddhi siddhisaṃpradaṃ

evaṃ yūyamapi jñātvā sarvadā śaraṇāśritāḥ|yathāśakti samabhyarcya bhajataitāṃstriratnikān

smṛtvā dhyātvāpi nāmāpi samuccārya sadā mudā|eṣāmeva triratnānāṃ bhajadhvaṃ śaraṇe sthitā

etatpuṇyaviliptā ye pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|bhadraśrīguṇasaṃpannā bhaveste śubhāśayā

durgatiṃ te na gaccheyuḥ sadā sadgatisaṃbhavāḥ|bodhisatvā mahāsatvā bhaveryubhadracāriṇaḥ

tataste sarvasatvānāṃ hitārtha sādhanodyatāḥ|sudhīrā bodhisambhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ

tato māragaṇāñjitvā niḥkleśā vijitendriyāḥ|arhanta trividhāṃ bodhiṃprāpya yāyu jinālayaṃ

etsarva mayā khyātaṃ sarvairapi munīśvaraiḥ|śrutvānumodanāṃ kṛtvā pracaradhvaṃ sadāśubhe

ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa niśamyate sabhāśritāḥ|sarve tathānumodantaḥ prāpyānandatpravodhitāḥ

iti śrī svayambhūtpattisamuddeśa mahāhrada śoṣaṇa dharmmadhātu padmagirisaṃprasthāpano nāma
tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ|
