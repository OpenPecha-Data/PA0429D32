navama adhyāyaḥśrīmahācārya śāntikaraguṇasaṃsiddhimahātmyānubhāva prakathanapravartano nāmaatha
maitreya ālokya samutthāya kṛtāñjaliḥ|bhagavantaṃ tamānamya prārthayaccaivamādarāt

kadā śāntikaraṃ nāma tasyābhavat kathaṃ punaḥ|taddhetuṃ śrotumicchāmi samupādeṣṭumarhati

iti saṃprārthite tena maitreyeṇa sa sarvavit|bhagavāstaṃ mahāsattvaṃ sampaśyannevamādiśat

śṛṇu maitreya vakṣyāmi śāntaśriyāṃ mahadguṇaṃ|saddharmmasādhanotsāhaṃ bhadraśrīsadgurṇārthadaṃ

yo'sau rājā mahāsattvaḥ sadharmmaguṇalālasaḥ|tyaktvā kāmasukhaṃ rājyaṃ tīrthayātrāmupācarat

sa sarveṣvapi tīrtheṣu snātvā dānaṃ vidhāya ca|trisamādhisamācāraḥ saṃcara poṣadhaṃ vrataṃ

evaṃ sarveṣu pīṭheṣu bhraman saddharmmamānasaḥ|yogacaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā pracacāra samāhitaḥ

puṇyakṣetreṣu sarveṣu bhramannaivamihāgatāḥ|dṛṣṭvaimaṃ maṇḍalaṃ ramye vismitaṃ samupāyayau

atraimaṃ dūrato dṛṣṭvā dharmmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|mudāṣṭāṅgaiḥ praṇatvāśuḥ saṃdraṣṭuṃ samupāyayau

sametya saṃmahāsattvā jyotīrūpaṃ jinālayaṃ|dharmmadhātumimaṃ dṛṣṭvā praṇatvā samupāśrayat

tataḥ samudito rājā nepāle'tra manorame|sarvatrāpi ca saṃdraṣṭuṃ pracacāra vilokayan

teṣu sarveṣu tīrtheṣu snātvā datvā yathepsitaṃ|yathāvidhi samādhāya vrataṃ cara samāhitaḥ

tatoṣṭau vītarāgāṃśca dṛṣṭvā sa saṃpramoditaḥ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcya sadābhajan

tataścāsau mahādevī khagānanāṃ maheśvarīṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajanmudā

tato mañjuśriyaścaityaṃ samīkṣya sa pramoditaḥ|yathāvidhi samabhyarcya prābhajat samupasthitaḥ

tataḥ ścaitatmahatpuṇyaiḥ śāntiśrīśubhitendriyaḥ|pravajyā saṃvaraṃ dhṛtvā brahmacārī babhūva
saḥ

yattasya supraśāntaśrīśobhitānīndriyāni ṣaṭ|taṃ nanāma prasiddhaṃ ca śāntaśrīrityabhūdyataḥ

tato'sau satmatirvijño bodhisattvo jagaddhite|vajracaryāvrataṃ gṛhaya pracacāra samāhitaḥ

tataśca śilayāchādya dharmmadhātuṃmimaṃ jinaṃ|iṣṭikābhirmahatstūpaṃ vidhāya samagopayat

tataḥ pañcapureṣvatra sthāpitā pañca devatāḥ|mañjuśrīyāmidaṃ caityamanena ca mahatkṛtaṃ

evaṃ kṛtvātra kāryāṇi sarvāṇi sa mahāmatiḥ|bodhisattvā mahābhijñaḥ pracacāra jagaddhite

tataścātra mahatpātaṃ śamīkṛtya samaṃtataḥ|bhadraśrīmaṅgalotsāhaṃ na śāntaśrīḥssadā vyadhāt

tataścāsau mahābhijño durvṛṣṭi parivarttate|nāgarājān samārādhya suvṛṣṭi samacārayat

evaṃ sa triguṇābhijñāṃ mahotpāta praśāntikṛt|śubhaṃkara sadā tena śāntikare iti smṛtaḥ

īdṛgmantrī mahābhijño vajrācāryā mahāmatiḥ|samṛddhisiddhisaṃpannā na bhūtā na bhaviṣyati

evaṃ vidhāya sarvatra nirutpātaṃ śubhotsavaṃ|bodhisattva sa śāntaśrīstrailokyamahitobhavat

evamasya mahatpuṇyaṃ bhadraśrīguṇasādhanaṃ|vijñāya śaraṇaṃ gatvā savitavyaṃ śubhārthibhiḥ

yadyasya śaraṇaṃ gatvā śraddhayā samupāśritāḥ|yathāvidhi samārādhya bhajeyuḥ sarvadā mudā

te sarve vimalatmāno niḥkleśā vijitendriyāḥ|bhadraśrīguṇasaṃpattiṃ sarddhisiddhiṃ samāyayuḥ

ye ca tasya sadā smṛtvā dhyātvāpi ca samāhitāḥ|nāmāpi ca samuccārya bhajeyuḥ śraddhayā sadā

te'pi sarve vikalmāṣāḥ pariśuddhatrimaṇḍalāḥ|tadguṇaśrīsamāpannā bhaveyu bodhicāriṇaḥ

ityevaṃ tatmahatpuṇyaṃ vijñāya tadguṇārthinaḥ|tasyaiva śaraṇaṃ gatvā bhajantu te sadā mudā

ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa sarve'pi te sabhāśritāḥ|lokāstatheti vijñapya prābhyanandan prabodhitāḥ

evamasau mahāsattvo bhadraśrīsadguṇārthabhṛt||sarvasattvahitaṃ kṛtvā saṃtasthe suciraṃ tathā

tataḥ kāle gate rājā vṛddhobhijīrṇṇitendriyaḥ|niḥkleśo viratābhāgo dhyātvaiva samacintayat

ahaṃ vṛddhotijīrṇṇāṅgaḥ sthāsyāmyevaṃ kiyāccaraṃ|avaśyaṃ daivayogeṇa yāsyāmi maraṇaṃ dhruvaṃ

tadatrāhaṃ svaputrāya yūne lokānupālane|sābhiṣekamidaṃ rājyaṃ dātumarhāmi sāmprataṃ

iti dhyātvā sa bhūpālo narendradevamātmajaṃ|abhiṣiṃcya nṛpaṃ kṛtvā bodhayannevamaṃnvaśāt

rājan putra samādhāya dharmanītyā samācaran|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā saṃcarasva sadā śubhe

adyārabhyāsi sarveṣāṃ lokānāmadhipaḥ prabhuḥ|sarvadharmānuśāstā ca sarvasattvahitārthabhṛt

tadatra sakalān lokān dharmanītyānupālayan|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā saṃcaraṃsva sadā śubhe

ityanuśāsya tanputraṃ pitā sa bhavanispṛhaḥ|sarvaparigrahānstyaktvā vanaprasthasamāśrayan

tatrasthāsau mahābhijñaḥ pariśuddhitrimaṇḍalaḥ|samādhinihitaḥ svāntaḥ sasaṃcare bahmasaṃbaraṃ

tataḥ kāle gate mṛtyusamaye sa samāhitaḥ|triratnaṃ sa smaraṃstyaktvā dehaṃ yayau sukhāvatīṃ

tataḥ sa nṛpatī rājā narendradeva indravat|saṃbodhayan prayatnena sarvān lokānapālayat

so'pi rājā viśuddhātmā saddharmmaguṇalālasaḥ|śāntikaraṃ tamācārya sametya śaraṇaṃ yayau

tatra sa samupāśritya śāsturājñāṃ śirovahan|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā prācarat sarvadā śubhe

sadā sarveṣu tīrtheṣu snānaṃ kṛtvā yathāvidhi|pitrebhyaḥ pradadau piṇḍaṃmarthibhyo'pi yathepsitaṃ

tathāṣṭau vītarāgāṃśca kṣatralokādhipāmapi|yathāvidhi samārādhya samārcayat sa parvasuḥ

tathā ca śrīmahādevīṃ khagānanāṃ yathāvidhi|samārādhya samabhyarcya mahotsāhairmudābhajat

tathā mañjuśrīyaścaitye pṛcchāgresminnupāśrayat|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajat sadā

tathā vāyupure vāyudevatāḥ sagaṇā api|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajat sadā

tathā vahnipure vahnidevatāḥ sagaṇā api|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajat sadā

tathā nāgapure nāgadevatāḥ sagaṇā api|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyābhajat sadā

tathā vasupure devīṃ vasudhārāṃ samaṇḍalām|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyā sadābhajat

tathā śāntipure śrīmatsambaraṃ sagaṇaṃ jinaṃ|yathāvidhi samārādhya samabhyarcyā sadābhajat

tathā tasya dharmadhātoḥ sa narendraḥ samupāśritaḥ|yathāvidhi samārādhya prābhajan sarvadārcayan

evaṃ sa nṛpatī rājā saddharmaguṇalālasaḥ|triratnabhajanaṃ kṛtvā saṃprācarat sadā śubhe

evaṃ sa nṛpa eteṣu tīrthayātrādikarmasu|bodhayitvā prayatnena sarvān lokān yayā jayat

tathā sarve'pi te lokā dhṛtvā nṛpānuśāsanaṃ|eteṣu tīrthayātrādikarmasu saṃpracerire

tathāṣṭau vītarāgāṃśca devīṃ khagānanāmapi|pañcaitā devatāścāpi caityaṃ mañjuśriyo'pi ca

jagadīśaṃ jagannāthaṃ dharmadhātuṃ jinālayaṃ|yathāvidhi samabhyarcya prābhajanta sadā mudā

etaddharmānubhāvena sarvadātra sumaṅgalaṃ|nirutpātaṃ mahotsāhaṃ prāvarttata samantataḥ

evaṃ sa nṛpatī rājā narendra deva ātmanaḥ|bodhicaryāḥ vrataṃ dhṛtvā saṃpracara jagaddhite

parānapi tathā sarvān lokān yatnena bodhayan|bodhimārge samāyujya prācārayajjagaddhite

evaṃ sa indravad rāja bodhisattvā jagatprabhuḥ|sarvasattvahitaṃ kṛtvā tasthau ciraṃ śubhe raman

tataḥ śrīmān sa ācāryaḥ śāntikaro maharddhikaḥ|kṛtakṛtyaḥ pravṛddho'pi nirvātuṃ nābhivāṃchati

sarvasattvahitākāṃkṣī śāntipurāgratādhasi|dhyānāgāre mahoddāre yājanaika pramāṇike

āropya śrīmahojvāle cintāmaṇi mahādhvajaṃ|sa prājñaḥ sa mahāsattvo bodhisattvā jinātmajaḥ

samādhidhāraṇīvidyāyogadhyānasamāhitaḥ|sambodhipraṇidhiṃ dhṛtvā tasthau niścaramānasaḥ

yadā saddharmmahīṇe'tra lokapaṃca kaṣāyite|tadotthāya samādheḥ sa saddharmma deśayiṣyati

yadā yadātra satmitraḥ śāstrā vidyādhipā na hi|tadā tadā na sanmitraḥ śāstrāvidyādhipobhavan

sarvānlokān prayatnena nivārya pāpamārgataḥ|bodhimārge pratiṣṭhāpya cārayiṣyati saddharme

evaṃ dhyātvā sa ācāryaḥ śāntikaraḥ samādhibhṛt|sarvasattvahitārthena tasthau yogasamāhitaḥ

evaṃ sa triguṇācāryaḥ sarvasattvahitārthabhṛt|bodhisattvamahābhijña tiṣṭha tatra jagaddhita

ye tasya śaraṇaṃ gatvā smṛtvā dhyātvā samādarāt|nāmāpi ca samuccāryaṃ bhajanti śraddhayā sadā

te'pi sarve mahābhijñā bodhisattvā vicakṣaṇāḥ|bhadraśrīguṇasaṃpannā bhaviṣyanti sadā bhave

tataste vimalātmānaścaturbrahmavihāriṇaḥ|bodhicaryāvrataṃ dhṛtvā cariṣyanti jagaddhite

tataste bodhisaṃbhāraṃ pūrayitvā yathākramaṃ|arhanto bodhimāsādya prāpsyanti saugataṃ padaṃ

ye ca tadguṇamāhātmyaṃ śṛṇvanti śraddhayā mudā|te'pi tadguṇa saṃpatti saṃsiddhiṃ samavāpnuyuḥ

iti vijñāya vāñchanti yastasya guṇasaṃpada|te sadguṇamāhātmyaṃ śrotumarhati sādaraṃ

ityādiṣṭaṃ muṇīndrena śrutvā sarvasabhāśritāḥ|lokāstatheti saṃśrutya prābhyanandan prabodhitāḥ

iti śrīmahācāryaśāntikaraguṇasaṃsiddhimāhātmyānubhāvaprakathanapravṛtto nāmādhyāya navamaḥ|
